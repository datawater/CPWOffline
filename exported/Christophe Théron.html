Christophe Théron [1]

Christophe Théron,
a French computer chess programmer living in Guadeloupe. He started chess programming at the age of 17 in 1982 on a TRS-80 1.77 Mhz home computer [2]. Christophe is author of the chess engine Chess Tiger.
