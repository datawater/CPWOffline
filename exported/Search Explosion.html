Home * Search * Search Explosion

Edvard Munch - The Scream[1]

A Search Explosion or node count explosion refers to a huge effective branching factor in iterative deepening, most often caused by move ordering suddenly becomes worse due to new refutations found at higher depth, by re-searching the root after aspiration window fail-high or fail-low, and/or enabling too many extensions and checks in quiescence search.
