Jouni Uski,
a Finnish chess and computer chess aficionado. He is tester, collector of test-positions, frequent poster in computer chess forums, and long term member in CCC, with interests in all aspects of chess engines, tournaments and mate solving [1]. In 1996 he reported on the Human vs. Computer Match in Finland, 1996 [2].
