Virtual Kasparov,
a commercial, Kasparov licensees [2] chess program for Game Boy Advance and PlayStation, published by Titus Interactive S.A., France and based on a chess engine by Marc-François Baudot and Jean-Christophe Weill.

Description

Description given from MobyGames [3]: 

Virtual Kasparov is a chess game with thirty-one opponents of varying difficulty to face in a story mode. Victory over this multi-cultural group of chess players will lead you to Gary Kasparov himself. Various styles of boards are included along with several 3D boards. Multiplayer is supported via link-cable or on a single system.
