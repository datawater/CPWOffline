Ioannis Antonoglou [1]

Ioannis Alexandros Antonoglou,
a Greek computer scientist and software engineer at Google DeepMind, involved in the AlphaGo and AlphaZero projects. He holds an engineer's degree in electrical and computer engineering from Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 2011, and an M.Sc. in artificial intelligence and machine learning from University of Edinburgh in 2012.
