Chess Monster,
a chess program by Jacques Middlecoff written in 8086 assembly, apparently successor of SPOC [2]. Chess Monster played the Halloween event, the WMCCC 1986 in Dallas running on a Compaq 386, fighting versus a strong field dominated by 6502 and 68020 based programs and three entries of Mephisto, Fidelity, Rebel and Cyrus 68K each [3].
