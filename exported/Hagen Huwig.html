Hagen Huwig [1]

Hagen Huwig,
a German mathematician and computer scientist with research interests in computational complexity theory. While Ph.D. student at University of Dortmund during the mid 70s, he led a project team to develop the chess program Proscha [2] [3], written in PL/I for the IBM 370. Proscha played the First GI Computer Chess Tournament [4] [5].
