Hermen Reitsma [1]

Hermen Reitsma,
a Dutch senior software engineer with long time experience in designing and implementing IT solutions and vast experience with agile methodologies. He holds a computer science degree from University of Twente in 2000, and was affiliated with DGT from 2001 to 2007. Along with Erik van het Hof, he is co-author of the chess engines BugChess aka QueenMaxima [2] and Maxima2.
