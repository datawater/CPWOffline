Home * Tournaments * International CSVN Tournament

From 2001 until 2013 the Dutch Computer Chess Federation (CSVN) organized the International CSVN Tournaments. Also known as the ICT, the CSVN spring tournament. Along with the Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship in fall, both were superseded by the CSVN Programmers Tournament.
