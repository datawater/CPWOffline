Shredder versus Hydra 2004,
was a 8 game computer chess match between Shredder and Hydra, from August 14 to 24, 2004 in Abu Dhabi during the 14th Abu Dhabi International Chess Festival [1]. Hydra won games 1, 2 and 7 while the five remaining games ended in a draw for a final 5½ - 2½ score for Hydra.
