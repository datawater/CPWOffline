Thomas Hubert [1]

Thomas Hubert,
a French computer scientist and research engineer at Google DeepMind, involved in the AlphaGo Zero and AlphaZero projects mastering the games of Go, chess and Shogi. He holds an engineering diplom from École Centrale Paris in 2009, and a M.Sc. in financial mathematics from Stanford University in 2010 [2].
