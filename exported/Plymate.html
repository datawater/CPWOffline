1986 Playmate of the Year [1]

Plymate,
a series of chess programs by Ulf Rathsman and Lars Hjörth written in Assembly to run on 6502 based dedicated chess computers with up to 8 MHz. Plymate was marketed as cartridge for Conchess computers [2], and was further incorporated inside the Mephisto MM II by Hegener & Glaser [3]. Plymate played four ICGA world computer chess tournaments, the WMCCC 1985, WCCC 1986, the WMCCC 1987 and the commercial group of the WMCCC 1988.
