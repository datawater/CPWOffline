Ayito,
a WinBoard and later UCI and Chess960 compliant chess engine by Jaime Benito de Valle Ruiz, written in C++, first released in May 2004 [1]. Ayito played the Chess960CWC 2005 in Mainz, the DOCCC 2005 in Leiden and the CPT 2008 in Hoogstraten-Wortel.
