Dieter Schultze,
a German electrical engineer and former chess programmer. While affiliated with VEB Mikroelektronik „Karl Marx“ Erfurt, Dieter Schultze developed the program of the early dedicated Chess-Master computers along with Rüdiger Worbs [1] [2], which participated at the 3rd World Microcomputer Chess Championship 1983 in Budapest [3].
