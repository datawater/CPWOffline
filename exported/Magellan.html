Ferdinand Magellan [1]

Magellan,
a chess program by Karl Pevny, written in C for a program size of only 64 KiB running under MS-DOS [2]. Magellan competed at the WMCCC 1993 in Munich, and gained respectable 4 out of 9 points [3].
