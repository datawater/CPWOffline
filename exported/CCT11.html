The CCT11 was organized by Peter Skinner, played via the Internet Chess Club (ICC) [1] , March 21-22, 2009, 9 rounds Swiss, 50min 3sec increment. After Rybka's disqualification in 2011, Fruit by Fabien Letouzey and Bright by Allard Siemelink are co-champions [2].
