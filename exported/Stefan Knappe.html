Stefan Knappe,
a German economist, club chess player [2] and avocational computer chess programmer. He is author of the chess program Matador, which also participated as Lokalmatador [3] at the BELCT 2001 in Berlin. Along with Dieter Bürßner et al., Stefan was member of the ChessCoach - Team, developing chess training software for Palm and Pocket-PC [4], also affiliated with the Inside Chess project focusing on web development [5].
