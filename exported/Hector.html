Hector,
a chess program by Maarten Bults, written in Turbo Pascal to run on a PC [2]. Hector played five consecutive Dutch Open Computer Chess Championships from 1992 until 1996, and three Aegon Tournaments, Aegon 1995, Aegon 1996, and Aegon 1997.
