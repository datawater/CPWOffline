Dragon,
a chess program by Yuri Shpeer, not to confused with the Chess Assistant Dragon analysis engine, which is based on Centaur by Victor Vikhrev and Alexey Manjakhin along with some code units incorporated from Mirage by Vladimir Rybinkin and Yuri Shpeer [2]. Dragon played the WMCCC 1995 in Paderborn, as well the Aegon 1996 and with 4/6, the Aegon 1997 human machine tournaments.
