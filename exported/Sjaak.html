Sjaak [1], (TrojanKnight)
a private chess, Antichess, and later Atomic Chess playing program by Ronald de Man, registered under the handle TrojanKnight [2] at FICS [3] [4]. Sjaak has a parallel SMP search [5], and was test bed of Ronald de Man's Syzygy Bases [6].
