GK 2100, (Saitek Kasparov GK 2100)
a dedicated chess computer released in 1993 by Saitek, running a program by Frans Morsch on an 8-bit H8 processor with 32 KB ROM and 1 KB RAM. The GK 2100 played the Aegon 1995 and the FCCC 1995.
