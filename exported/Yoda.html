Master Yoda [1]

Yoda,
a winboard engine by Daniel Anulliero, written in C, announced in June 2008 [2]. After he made first WinBoard experience with his engine JARS, Yoda was Daniel Anulliero's second attempt, where the final version 2.09 introduced an hash table bug [3]. Yoda 1.30 and Yoda 2.09 (bug-fixed) are available from Ron Murawski's download site [4].
