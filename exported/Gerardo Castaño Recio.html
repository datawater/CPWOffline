Gerardo Recio [1]

Gerardo Castaño Recio,
a Spanish medical doctor [2] at the Health Center of La Puebla de Montalbán, Province of Toledo. As avocational programmer, he is author of Salud 2000 (Health 2000), a program to manage cardiovascular risk factors, and author of the chess programs Zeus, Toledo 2000 and Caligula. All of his three engine names were later used by other programmers namesake!
