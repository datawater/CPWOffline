Piazza San Benedetto, Norcia [1] [2]

The I.G.T. 2016 (International Gsei Tournament) was planned to be held in Norcia Ospitalia Il Capisterium, Norcia, Perugia, Italy on September 10-11, 2016. Due to the earthquake which struck Norcia on August 24, 2016 [3] [4], the IGT was held at Accademia Scacchistica Romana [5] , Rome [6] .
