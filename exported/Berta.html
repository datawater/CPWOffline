Dicke Berta [1]

Berta,
a private chess engine [2] written in C/C++ by Felix Schmenger. Berta regularly plays online at the Internet Chess Club [3], and has participated at various online tournaments, such as CCT tournaments and ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championships, most recently the WCRCC 2018.
