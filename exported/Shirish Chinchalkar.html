Shirish Chinchalkar [1]

Shirish Chinchalkar,
an Indian American mathematician, mechanical engineer, computer scientist, and senior analyst at Moody's Investors Service [2]. He holds a Ph.D. in mechanical engineering from Cornell University and an M.A. in mathematical finance from New York University, and gained experience in numerical and high-performance computing, and computational finance, where he applied Monte Carlo methods, numerical optimization, and parallel computing while teaching and researching at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay and Cornell University [3].

Number of Chess Positions

In 1996, Shirish Chinchalkar determined 10^46.25 as upper bound for the number of reachable chess positions [4].
