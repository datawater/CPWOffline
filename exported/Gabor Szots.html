Gábor at MASPV 2004 [1]

Gábor Szõts,
a Hungarian chess player [2], and as tester of chess programs, member of the CCRL group. He helped with the Hungarian translation for Arena and also served as tournament organizer at various Hungarian Chessprogram Open.
