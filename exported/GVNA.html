GVNA, (GVNAA, GVNAC, GVNAF)
a series of mainframe chess programs written in the early 80s by Gert Vriend in Fortran. Development and target platform was a PDP-10 at Wageningen University [1]. GVNAA played the DOCCC 1981, GVNAC the DOCCC 1982 and GVNAF the DOCCC 1983.
