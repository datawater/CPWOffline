Harry Schnapp [1]

Harry Schnapp, (died on May 05, 2012)
was a German renowned author of opening books, public releases and suits as used for various Engine Rating Lists, as well as dedicated books for Rybka and Equinox competing the WCCC 2009 in Pamplona [2]. Harry Schnapp died on May 5, 2012 at age 77 [3].
