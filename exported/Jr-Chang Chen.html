Jr-Chang Chen

Jr-Chang Chen,
a Taiwanese computer scientist, game researcher and programmer, and associate professor at Chung Yuan Christian University in Zhongli City, Taoyuan County. Jr-Chang Chen received his B.Sc., M.Sc., and Ph.D. degrees in CS and information engineering from National Taiwan University, in 1996, 1998, and 2005, respectively [1]. He is co-author of the Chinese Chess programs and multiple gold medal winners at Computer Olympiads, Contemplation, ELP, and more recently, Chimo [2], the Shogi program EVG, and the Nurikabe puzzle program Enigma [3].
