Bruce C. Wright,
an American computer scientist, programmer and independent computer software professional [1]. As graduate student at the Duke University in the 70s, Bruce Wright was early chess programmer and along with Tom Truscott and Eric Jensen, co-author of the chess program Duchess, which competed at ACM North American Computer Chess Championships from 1974 to 1979 and 1981, and the 2nd and 3rd World Computer Chess Championship in Toronto 1977 and Linz 1980.
