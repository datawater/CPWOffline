The venue of the Championships and Conference [1]

The Sixth International Conference on Computers and Games was held from September 29 to October 1, 2008 at Beijing Golden Century Golf Club, Qinglonghu Township, Fangshan District, Beijing, China, in conjunction with the 13th Computer Olympiad and the 16th World Computer Chess Championship .
