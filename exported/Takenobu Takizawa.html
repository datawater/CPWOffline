Takenobu Takizawa [1]

Takenobu Takizawa,
a Japanese mathematician, computer scientist, and professor in mathematics at the Faculty of Political Science and Economics, Waseda University, Shinjuku, Tokyo. His research interests include fuzzy theory, mathematical game theory, and artificial intelligence, in particular, applied to computer Shogi. Takenobu Takizawa is chair of the Computer Shogi Association (CSA), organizer of World Computer Shogi Championship and the Game Programming Workshops [2].
