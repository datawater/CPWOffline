Tom Vijlbrief [1]

Tom Vijlbrief,
a Dutch computer scientist and senior IT Architect. He holds a M.Sc. degree in 1986 from University in Amsterdam [2]. As a computer chess programmer, Tom Vijlbrief is author of the chess program Ant [3], which regularly participated at Dutch Open Computer Chess Championships and International CSVN Tournaments. More recently, Tom contributed to the Stockfish development [4], in particular to the Fishtest framework [5].
