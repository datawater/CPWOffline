Daisaku Yokoyama,
a Japanese computer scientist, Shogi programmer, and research associate at University of Tokyo with interests in parallel computing, programming languages, robot programming, game-tree search, and computer-shogi [1]. Daisaku Yokoyama is co-author of the Shogi engine Gekisashi, Gold medal winner of the 15th Computer Olympiad, Shogi in Kanazawa.
