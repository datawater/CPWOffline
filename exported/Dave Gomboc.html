David (Dave) Ross Gomboc,
a Canadian computer scientist and AI researcher affiliated with AAAI, ACM, ICGA and IEEE [1] and the University of Southern California [2]. His research interests include automatic tuning of evaluation functions, which was also topic of his 2004 masters thesis at University of Alberta [3]. In 2000, Dave Gomboc serverd as moderator in CCC.
