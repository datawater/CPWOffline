European Robin [1]

Robin,
a chess program developed by Péter Horváth since 1991, written in C [2], running under MS-DOS. Robin played the MASPV 2001, but was superseded by Péter's WinBoard engine Asterisk at subsequent Magyar Sakkprogramok Versenye since MASPV 2002.
