McTobber,
an experimental version of MacChess by Wim van Beusekom. McTobber averages over 400,000 nps on an iMac DV, G3 [1], with PowerPC 750 processor @ 400MHz, 16 MiB of RAM [2], and played the DOCCC 1999 and the DOCCC 2000.
