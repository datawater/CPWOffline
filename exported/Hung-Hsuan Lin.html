Hung-Hsuan Lin, (Hung-Xuan Lin)
a Taiwanese computer scientist at Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu. His research interests include computer games and machine learning. He is co-author of the Chinese Chess program Chimo, and the Connect6 program NCTU6-Lite, Gold medal winner of the 13th Computer Olympiad [1].
