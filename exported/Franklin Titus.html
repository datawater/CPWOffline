Franklin Titus (kingliveson) [1],
a computer chess aficionado and programmer, opening book author, expert of the Ippolit family of engines, hosting RobboLito and IvanHoe sources and executables, as well as various endgame tablebases and opening books [2] [3]. Franklin Titus was early critic of Robert Houdart concerning Houdini and its apparent RobboLito origins [4]. He was active poster in CCC until mid 2010, and since then in Jeremy Bernstein's OpenChess Forum.
