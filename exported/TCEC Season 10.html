TCEC Season 10,
the Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC) Season 10 started on October 14, 2017. It was a competition between 24 chess engines in Stage 1, playing a single round robin, timecontrol 60' + 10" - 8 will qualify for Stage 2. The Superfinal was won with 53-47 by Houdini 6.03 versus Komodo 1970.00
