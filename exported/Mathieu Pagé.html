Mathieu Pagé,
a Canadian software devloper in the insurance induytry and as avocational computer chess programmer author of the Chess Engine Communication Protocol compliant chess engine MatMoi [1], under development approximately since 2003. MatMoi 7.15.0, which played the CCT12, was published as free engine in March 2010 [2].
