Snellius Building [1]

21st World Computer Chess Championship 2015, from June 29, to July 03, 2015, Snellius Building [2] [3], Leiden University, Leiden, The Netherlands, in conjunction with the WCSC 2015, Advances in Computer Games 14 [4], and the 18th Computer Olympiad. Jonny, by Johannes Zwanzger, brought home the gold on the 2400 core cluster, thanks to upset victories against Komodo in round 5 and HIARCS in round 9. Komodo is second and third place is shared by Hiarcs and Protector [5].
