Computerschach und Spiele (CSS), a German computer chess periodical. From 1983 until 2005 it was a paper magazine by Dieter Steinwender and Frederic Friedel. Since 2005 CSS is available online with Steinwender as sole publisher and editor in chief [1]. The scanned CSS issues from 1983 until 2000 were available as DVD from Steinwender EDV-Beratung [2] [3] [4].
