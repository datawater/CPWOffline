Olivier Deville [1]

Olivier Deville,
a French visually impaired [2] chess player, chess organizer and arbiter [3], computer chess tester, and from 2003 until 2012 organizer of the prestigious ChessWar, OpenWar and AEGT engine-engine tournaments. Since May 2008, Olivier Deville administrates the Winboard Forum [4].
