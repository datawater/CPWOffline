CCR One Hour Test by Larry Kaufman, published 1994 in Computer Chess Reports [1] [2]. The test was still used in 2018 by Marco Wiering et al. in their deep learning experiments to appraise the performance of a neural networks evaluator with a lookahead of depth one [3].
