Peter Doggers [1]

Peter Doggers,
a Dutch chess player and chess journalist, Master of Arts in Dutch language and literature [2]. Between 2007 and 2013 Peter Doggers was running ChessVibes, in 2013 acquired by Chess.com [3]. Peter Doggers continued his work as chess journalist at Chess.com. His postings include multiple computer chess topics.
