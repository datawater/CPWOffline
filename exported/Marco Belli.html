Marco Belli,
an Italian computer chess programmer and author of the UCI compliant chess engines Vajolet and Vajolet2. After early chess programming trials in C#, Marco worked through Stef Luijten's tutorial on Winglet, Writing a Chess Program in 99 Steps [1], and created his second engine in C++, already forerunner of Vajolet2 [2].
