Casa Consistorial de Bilbao [1]

The Man vs Machine Team Championship, October 6 to 9, 2004, and its follow up, the People vs Computers World Chess Team Match, November 20 to 23, 2005, both held in Bilbao, Spain, were sanctioned by the ICGA [2] . The organization was led by Andoni Madariaga, contacts to the chess world (FIDE) and the computer chess world (ICGA) were performed by Leontxo Garcia. The arbitrary selection of the three programs raised some discussions [3] .

Man vs Machine Team Championship 2004

Hydra, Fritz 8 and Deep Junior played Veselin Topalov, Ruslan Ponomariov and Sergey Karjakin [4] .

Silicon / Carbon Ponomariov Karjakin Topalov Sum Silicon
Hydra 1w 1b 1w ½b 3½
Fritz 8 ½b 1w 1b 1w 3½
Deep Junior ½w 0b ½w ½b 1½
Sum Carbon 1 1 1½ 8½ - 3½

People vs Computers World Chess Team Match 2005

Hydra, Deep Junior and Fritz 9 played Ruslan Ponomariov, Rustam Kasimdzhanov and Alexander Khalifman.

Silicon / Carbon Kasimdzhanov Ponomariov Khalifman Sum Silicon
Hydra 1w ½b 1w ½b 3
Deep Junior ½b 1w ½b 1w 3
Fritz 9 ½w 0b 1w ½b 2
Sum Carbon 1½ 1½ 1 8 - 4
