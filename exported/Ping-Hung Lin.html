Ping-Hung Lin,
a Taiwanese computer scientist at Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu. His research interests include computer games, grid and cloud computing and machine learning. He is co-author of the the gold medal winning Connect6 program NCTU6-Lite, and holds his Ph.D. thesis on the topic of Relevance-Zone-Oriented Proof Search for Connect6 under supervision of I-Chen Wu [1] .
