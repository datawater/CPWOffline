Markus Gille [1]

Markus Gille,
a German chess programmer and chess player [2], along with Ernst A. Heinz and Peter Gillgasch, author of the computer chess program DarkThought. Beside programming on DarkThought, Markus was also responsible of its opening book - and represented and operated DarkThought at various tournaments.
