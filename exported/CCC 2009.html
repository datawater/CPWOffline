The 4th Chess Computer Cup, November 7 and 8, 2009, Carugate, Italy. Six rounds Swiss, timecontrol 60 minutes + 10 seconds increment per move. The event was organized by Accademia Scacchistica Le Due Torri [1] , Bologna, the Department of Computer Science – University of Bologna, and the Associazione Italiana di Calcolo Automatico (AICA) [2] .
