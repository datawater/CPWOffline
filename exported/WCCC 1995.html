Ho Sin Hang Engineering Building, CUHK [1]

The Eighth World Computer Chess Championship took place from May 25-30, 1995, Ho Sin Hang Engineering Building at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shatin, Hong Kong - China.

Fritz on a 90 MHz P5 won the title after a play-off versus Star Socrates, after already winning again Deep Blue Prototype in round five.
