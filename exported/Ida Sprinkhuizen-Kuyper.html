Ida Sprinkhuizen-Kuyper [1]

Ida Gerda Sprinkhuizen-Kuyper,
a Dutch applied mathematician and professor at F.C. Donders Centre for Cognitive Neuroimaging, Radboud University Nijmegen, since 2014 retired. Her research interests include artificial intelligence, especially machine learning and data mining, neural networks, evolutionary algorithms, multi-agent systems and swarm intelligence, and robots.
