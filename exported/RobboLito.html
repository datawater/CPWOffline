RobboLito,
an UCI compliant chess engine of the Ippolit series of programs. RobboLito versions were further developed by Norman Schmidt and Sentinel, attached with the GNU GPL. Due to the circumstances that Houdini 1.0 by Robert Houdart is apparently based on the GPL licensed RobboLito 0.085g3 [2] [3] , and the Espin case with the SquarknII clone [4] their Robbolito versions are no longer available as source code from the ChessLogik sites [5] .
