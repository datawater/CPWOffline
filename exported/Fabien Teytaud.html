Fabien Teytaud [1]

Fabien Teytaud,
a French computer scientist and associate professor at University of the Littoral Opal Coast (ULCO). He defended his Ph.D. at Paris-Sud 11 University in 2011 under the direction of Marc Schoenauer and Olivier Teytaud, and was member of the TAO Project Team [2] of LRI. His research interests in the fields of artificial intelligence and machine lerning include evolutionary algorithms, global and stochastic optimization, reinforcement learning and computer games. He is co-author of various papers on Parallel Monte-Carlo Tree Search. Along with Olivier Teytaud, he is co-author of the Havannah programs Shakti and Shakty.
