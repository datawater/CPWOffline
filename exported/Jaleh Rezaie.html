Jaleh Rezaie [1]

Jaleh Rezaie,
an American computer scientist and academic administrator, associate provost & dean of graduate studies at North Carolina Central University, and before, until 2015, associate dean of graduate education & research and director of the STEM-H institute (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics and Health) at Eastern Kentucky University [2]. Jaleh Rezaie defended her Ph.D. in 1993 at the University of Kentucky with a thesis on distributed game-tree search under the supervision of Raphael Finkel [3].

Selected Publications

  • Jaleh Rezaie, Raphael Finkel (1992). A comparison of some parallel game-tree search algorithms. University of Kentucky
  • Jaleh Rezaie(1993). Distributed game-tree search. Ph.D. thesis, University of Kentucky [4]
