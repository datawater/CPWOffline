Thomas Philip Runarsson [1]

Thomas Philip Runarsson,
an Icelandic computer scientist a professor within the School of Engineering and Natural Sciences, at the University of Iceland. His research interests include evolutionary computation, global optimization, games, approximated dynamic programming, statistical learning, heuristics and real world applications.
