Igor Kononenko [1]

Igor Kononenko,
a Slovenian computer scientist and professor at Faculty of Computer and Information Science as well as head of the Laboratory for Cognitive Modeling [2] at University of Ljubljana. His research interests covers artificial intelligence, machine learning, neural networks and cognitive modeling.
