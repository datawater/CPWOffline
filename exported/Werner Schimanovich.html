Werner Schimanovich, (Werner DePauli-Schimanovich, Werner-Jimmy-DeViena DePauli-Schimanovich-Göttig)
an Austrian mathematician, computer scientist, mechanical engineer, artist, composer, and Ph.D. Professor at Vienna University of Technology [2] . He studied and researched at Vienna University of Technology and University of Vienna, where his interests include automata theory, formal languages, artificial intelligence, combinatorial game theory and mathematical logic as well their implementation in logic programming and transport engineering, and the biographic research of the life and work of Kurt Gödel. , and is further interested in arts, psychotherapy, hypnosis and psychoanalysis.

Computer Chess Supporter

DePauli-Schimanovich-Göttig is not only inventor of various games and puzzles, but also did the game programming to run the games on a computer. At Vienna University of Technology in the early 80s he was along with Wilhelm Barth supporter of the Merlin team around Hermann Kaindl, Helmut Horacek and Marcus Wagner, and gives some details on this time in his sixth issue of his work Europolis [3] .
