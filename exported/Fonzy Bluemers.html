Fonzy Bluemers [1]

Fons (Fonzy) Bluemers,
a Dutch software developer, computer chess aficionado, tester, and computer chess programmer. He worked on and improved PolyGlot, and is along with Pradu Kannan and Andres Valverde co-author of the chess engine Dirty, the project which was born after a chat at his Dirty Dozen broadcast [2], Fonzy working actively on core engine structures and search [3].
