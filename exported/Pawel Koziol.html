Pawel Koziol [1]

Pawel Koziol,
an amateur chess programmer from Poland. Author of Hopeless (open-source, written in Delphi, characterized by very unbalanced playing style) and CCCP (free but closed source, derived from TSCP, playing also chess variant called knightmate). Pawel Koziol has contributed to the Chess Programming Wiki mainly to the pages on evaluation. He is co-author ofthe CPW-engine and is author of Glass [2], and the more recent open source engine Rodent [3].
