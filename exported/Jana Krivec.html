Jana Krivec [1]

Jana Krivec,
a Slovenian chess woman grandmaster, computer scientist and researcher at Department of Intelligent systems [2], Jožef Stefan Institute, Ljubljana. Previously, she was affiliated with the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory [3] at University of Ljubljana. Her research interests covers machine learning and automated explanation / tutoring systems.
