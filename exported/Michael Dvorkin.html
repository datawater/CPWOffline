Michael Dvorkin,
a Lithuanian-born American computer scientist with a degree from Vilnius University, software developer, señor engineer, and chess player [3]. He has over 20 years of software engineering experience spanning six Silicon Valley startups focused on bringing new technologies and products to market. He is founder and original developer of Fat Free CRM [4] [5], inventor of the Ante [6] esoteric programming language, author of the awesome_print Ruby gem [7], and the Donna chess engine, written in the Go programming language [8] .
