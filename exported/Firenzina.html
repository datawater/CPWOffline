Firenzina,
an UCI compliant open source chess engine by Norman Schmidt, Dmitri Gusev and Matthew R. Brades [2]. It is a derivative of Fire 2.2 xTreme by Norman Schmidt, and a derivative (via Fire) of FireBird by Norman Schmidt and Milos Stanisavljevic [3], a derivative (via Fire and FireBird) based on Ippolit engines by Yakov Petrovich Golyadkin et al..
