Norbert Raimund Leisner,
a German computer chess aficionado, collector of own compiled chess engines and tester. He serves as editor in Ron Murawski's Computer-Chess Wiki [1], and is frequent poster in Computer Chess Forums, mostly providing informations on chess engines and engine updates.
