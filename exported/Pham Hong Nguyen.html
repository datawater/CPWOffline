Nguyen Pham

Pham Hong Nguyen, (given name is Nguyen)
a Vietnamese scientist working on AI and NLP compiler fields. Chess programming is his main hobby. He has developed some programs for both chess and Chinese Chess (Xiangqi). He is author of the Banksia CLI Tournament Manager, and the Graphical User Interface Banksia GUI.
