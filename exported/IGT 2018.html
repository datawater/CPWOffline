The I.G.T. 2018 (International Gsei Tournament) was held on October 27 and 28, 2018 in Borgo Virgilio, Mantua, Lombardy. During the tournament, a two game man vs machine match between FIDE master Valerio Luciani [2] and AdaChess took place, both games won by AdaChess [3].
