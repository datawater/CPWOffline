The Fourth Livingston [1] Chess960 Computer World Championship was held within the scope of the Chess Classic Mainz, Germany, July 30 - August 1, 2008, organized by Hans-Walter Schmitt and his Chess Tigers [2] . The two finalists from 2007, Rybka and Shredder were challenged by two qualifiers from an online tournament on Internet Chess Club (ICC) [3], Naum and Deep Sjeng. Hans Secelle served as arbiter, and Eric van Reem as Tournament director.
