TCEC Season 2,
the second TCEC season directed and organized by Martin Thoresen, held from February 11, 2011 until April 21, 2011. Houdini 1.5a defended its title and won the nTCEC Season 2 Superfinal with a margin of 22-18 versus Rybka 4.1.
