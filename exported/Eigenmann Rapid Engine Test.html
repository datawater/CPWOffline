The Eigenmann Rapid Engine Test (ERET), designed by Walter Eigenmann, is a collection of 111 test-positions covering a wide range of chess motives with unique solutions. ERET was published in the Glarean Magazin in March 2017 [1]. The number of correct solutions in 15 seconds per position is the result to roughly compare engine skills.
