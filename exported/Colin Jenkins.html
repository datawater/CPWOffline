Colin Jenkins,
a British scientific software developer from the Mumbles district of Swansea, Wales. He holds a B.Sc. with Honours in mathematics and computer science in 1982 from the University of South Wales, and a M.Sc. in cognition, computing & psychology in 1984 from University of Warwick. While professionally working in the fields of artificial intelligence, cognitive science and psychology, massively parallel hardware architectures, robotics, and signal processing, he cares about public health and is interested in infectious disease modelling, and further started chess programming with his JavaScript engine Lozza [1] [2].
