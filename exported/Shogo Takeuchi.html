Shogo Takeuchi [1]

Shogo Takeuchi,
a Japanese computer scientist, lecturer at School of Information, Kochi University of Technology, and previously postdoc at Graduate School of Frontier Sciences, the University of Tokyo. His research interests include performance evaluation, search and learning algorithms in the computer board games of Shogi, Chess, Go, and Othello [2].
