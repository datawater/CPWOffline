Sam Tannous [1]

Sam Tannous,
an American electrical engineer and systems software engineer at NVIDIA in Durham, North Carolina. He holds a BSEE from University of South Florida and a MSEE from Georgia Institute of Technology [1]. Sam Tannous is author of Shatranj, an open source computer chess toolkit and engine written in Python. In 2007, he introduced Hashing Dictionaries to determine sliding piece attacks [2].
