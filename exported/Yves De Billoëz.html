Yves De Billoëz [1]

Yves De Billoëz,
a Belgian ICT architect, manager and consultant with expertise in computer security, cryptography and banking operations [2]. He is CTO and Co-Founder of eSureCoin.com, Democratic Republic of the Congo, providing banks and financial institutions the ability to offer Bitcoin purchase and sale services [3]. As avocational computer chess programmer, Yves De Billoëz is author of the open source engine Belofte.
