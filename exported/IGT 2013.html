View of Omegna [1]

The I.G.T. 2013 (International Gsei Tournament) organized by the G 6 was held in Omegna, Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola (VB), Piedmont, Italy, on the side of Lago d’Orta, November 09-10, 2013. Only five original programs competed, hoping to attract more programmers the next editions.
