Elektro [1]

Elektro,
an UCI compliant chess engine by Roberto Munter alias Dr. Otto Schmidt, derived from Igorrit of the Ippolit series of programs, written for mere personal entertainment [2]. The Elektro Chess Engine site [3] introduces the engine as a parody of the pseudo Soviet propaganda of the pseudonym Ippolit authors and comrades [4].
