Jorge L. Ortega-Arjona [1]

Jorge Luis Ortega-Arjona,
a Mexican electrical engineer, computer scientist and professor at department of mathematics, National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). He holds a degree in electrical engineering in 1992, a M.Sc. in CS in 1996, both from UNAM, and a Ph.D. in CS from University College London, University of London in 2007 [2]. His research interests include software design pattern and parallel computing. Along with Manuel Cristóbal López-Michelone, Jorge Luis Ortega-Arjona introduced a pattern description language for chess positions, published in ICGA Journal, Vol. 42, No. 1 [3]:
