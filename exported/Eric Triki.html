Eric Triki [1]

Eric Triki,
a French mathematician, engineer in computer science and information technology, M.Sc. in operations research and pure mathematics, and associate professor of mathematics within Education Nationale [2]. As computer chess programmer, Eric Triki is author of the chess program E.T.Chess.
