Kuang-che Wu,
a Taiwanese computer scientist and Chinese Chess programmer. He holds a B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University. His research interests include endgame databases and search algorithms in Computer Chinese Chess. Kuang-che Wu is co-author of the Chinese Chess programs Contemplation and Elephant [1].
