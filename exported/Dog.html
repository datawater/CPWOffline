Dog,
an UCI compliant chess engine by Folkert van Heusden, first released in 2022. It is mainly targeted for running on ESP32 microcontrollers altough it can be compiled for Linux (and other OSes) as well. It is called Dog as the writer acquiered a dog. Initially, Dog was Micah with some parts removed and some things added. As time goes on the differences get bigger.
