Enigma Machine [1]

Enigma,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol compatible chess engine by Kamil Przybyła, written in C and first released in November 2003 [2]. Enigma is a quite selective searcher with the aim to reduce the effective branching factor below two, and focuses on pawn structure, specially passed pawns, and has some speculative king attack code. Enigma participated at the PCCC 2003.
