Excelsior, E Pluribus Unum [1]

Excelsior,
a WinBoard compliant chess engine by Marek Kłonowski, initially written in Delphi, and since version 3.16 rewritten in C# [2], which requires the Windows .Net framework. Excelsior (Delphi) played the PCCC 2003, released to the public in November 2004 [3], the C# version played the IOPCCC 2007.
