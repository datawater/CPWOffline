The Venetian Macau [1]

25th World Computer Chess Championship 2019,
from August 13 to 16, 2019, in The Venetian Macao, Macau, China in conjunction with the WCSC 2019, the 22nd Computer Olympiad and the Advances in Computer Games 16 conference, all organized by the ICGA, alongside with the 28th IJCAI conference [2]. Six participants played a double round robin. Komodo defended its title to became World Computer Chess Champion the fourth time in a row. The World Speed Computer Chess Championship was won by Jonny [3].
