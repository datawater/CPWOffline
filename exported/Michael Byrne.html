Michael B [1]

Michael (Mike) Byrne,
an American now retired certified public accountant and finance manager, in 1983 graduated from University of Pennsylvania [2], and further chess player and computer chess aficionado. He wrote computer chess articles for Computer Chess Reports in the 90s, and served as moderator in CCC. He is member of the Crafty team around Robert Hyatt. More recently, he plays around with a derivative of Senpai 1.0 by Fabien Letouzey, dubbed Kōhai [3] [4], the Gull 3 fork dubbed Hawkeye [5], and a Stockfish fork with Cerebellum book, dubbed McBrain [6] aka McCain, Burnzie [7] and Honey.
