Tessa Pijl,
a Dutch youth chess player and operator of her father's, Richard Pijl's engines Crashtest Dummy and The Baron at various tournaments, notably the ICT 2007, ICT 2013, the DOCCC 2007 and the CPT 2008, where Tessa acted not only as operator but also as host. More recently, she operated Arasan in various CSVN Programmers Tournaments, and The Baron at the WCCC 2015.
