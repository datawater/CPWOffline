Harvard University,
a private Ivy League research university in Cambridge, Massachusetts, United States. Established in 1636 and named for clergyman John Harvard, its history, influence, and wealth have made it one of the world's most prestigious universities. Harvard is the United States' oldest institution of higher learning [2].
