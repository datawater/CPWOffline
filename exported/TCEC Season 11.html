TCEC Season 11,
the Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC) Season 11 started on January 03, 2018. From this Season 11 TCEC is upgrading its format to a league system with promotions and relegations [2] . The Superfinal between Stockfish 260318 and Houdini 6.03 was won by Stockfish with 59-41.
