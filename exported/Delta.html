Delta,
a chess program by Fré Felkers written in Turbo Pascal [2]. Delta played eleven Dutch Open Computer Chess Championships in the period from 1985 [3] to 1998, best result in 1991 with 2½ out of 7, as well the WMCCC 1990 in Lyon and the IPCCC 1991.
