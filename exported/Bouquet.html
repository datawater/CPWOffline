Bouquet,
an UCI compliant Ippolit derivative served as closed source by Jose Mº Velasco [2]. Bouquet was first released in May 2012 [3], and required MSVC++ 2010 dlls [4]. 32- and 64-bit executables for respective Windows versions were released - currently no longer available. Bouquet 1.8 has a parallel search which supports up to 16 threads, and uses runtime-detection whether the x86 or x86-64 population count instruction is available or not [5] .
