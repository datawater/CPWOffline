Caligula [1]

Caligula,
a chess program by Gerardo Castaño Recio and successor of Toledo 2000. Caligula played a strong 6th Spanish Open Computer Chess Championship 1999 and became Spanish Champion and runner-up behind Chess Tiger, winning from Tiger [2] and Diep, but losing from its countrymen programs Eugen and Ruy Lopez.
