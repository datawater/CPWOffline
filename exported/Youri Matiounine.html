Youri Matiounine,
an American actuary at New York Life Insurance Company [1]. As computer games programmer, Youri Matiounine is author of the Othello bot Ymatioun [2], open source under the GNU GPL v3 [3], written in C++ using AVX2 intrinsics and some x86-64 assembly with AVX2 instructions, and the Chess Engine Communication Protocol and later UCI compliant chess engine Fizbo, introduced in May 2014 to CCRL [4], which became open source in October 2019 [5] [6].
