Cyborg,
a chess program by Juul Moormann, initially written 1981 in Z80 assembly for the TRS-80, later ported to 8086 assembly, to run on an IBM PC. The PC version Cyborg 1.0 made its debut at the DOCCC 1983 in Leiden with a 50% score [2].
