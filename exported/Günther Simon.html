Günther Simon, (Guenther Simon)
a German chess player [1], computer chess aficionado, computer chess tester and Opening Book Author, notably for Peter Fendrich's TerraPi aka Alaric at the WCRCC 2007 and WCRCC 2008. Since 2001 Günther Simon hosts and maintains the Regensburg WinBoard Circuit (RWBC), until 2010 a rating list site, after a break since 2016 reactivated, providing the XB/UCI chronology of engines capable of using etiher the Chess Engine Communication Protocol (XBoard, WinBoard or UCI [2], computer chess links and engine downloads [3].
