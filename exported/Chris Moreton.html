Chris Moreton [1]

Chris Moreton,
a British computer scientist, B.Sc. in Computing and Informatics from Plymouth University in 1996, software and web application developer. Along with Russell Newman, Moreton has developed and maintains the Red Hot Pawn correspondence chess site [2], and is co-author of the chess engine Rival [3].
