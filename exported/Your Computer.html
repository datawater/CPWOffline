Your Computer,
a British computer magazine published monthly from 1981 to 1988 with focus on home computers [2], such as Sinclair ZX81, ZX Spectrum, Commodore VIC-20, Commodore 64, Acorn Atom, and BBC Micro, with occasional computer chess articles.
