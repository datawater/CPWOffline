Velvet, (Velvet Chess Engine)
an UCI compatible open source chess engine by Martin Honert, written in Rust, licensed under the GPL v3.0. The first release in August 2020 was based on the author's previous web-based engine Wasabi [2]. Velvet v2.0.0, released in July 2021, features NNUE evaluation along with magic bitboards [3].
