Kyōsei-kan [1]

The 8th International Computers and Games Conference, Keio University Kyōsei-kan, the Collaboration Complex [2], Yokohama, Japan, August 13-15, 2013. CG 2013 was held in conjunction with the WCCC 2013, WCSC 2013, and the 17th Computer Olympiad.
