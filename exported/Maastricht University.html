Important UM Dates

  • Until 1996 the University of Limburg in Maastricht [2]
  • In 1996 name change to 'Universiteit Maastricht' [3]
  • 11 January 2008: Name change to 'Maastricht University'
  • Until 2008 associated with the Editorial Board of the ICGA Journal [4].
