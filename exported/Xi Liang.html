Xi Liang (梁熙),
a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Department of Computer Science at National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu. In 2015, he defended his master's degree in CS on the topic of solving Hex openings using Job-level UCT (JL-UCT) [1], further published along with Ting-Han Wei and I-Chen Wu in the ICGA Journal [2].

Selected Publications

[3]

  • Xi Liang (2015). Job-Level Search for Solving Hex. Master's thesis, National Chiao Tung University, advisor I-Chen Wu
  • Xi Liang, Ting-Han Wei, I-Chen Wu (2015). Job-level UCT search for solving Hex. CIG 2015
  • Xi Liang, Ting-Han Wei, I-Chen Wu (2015). Solving Hex Openings Using Job-Level UCT Search. ICGA Journal, Vol. 38, No. 3
