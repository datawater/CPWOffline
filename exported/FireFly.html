FireFly, (Firefly)
an UCI and WinBoard compliant chess engine by Andrew Fan, first released in May 2004. FireFly is written in C, and applies an own 32-bit implementation of single line Magic Bitboards aka Kindergarten Bitboards [2], is able to probe Nalimov Tablebases, and performs a parallel search.
