Praetorians Relief [1]

Praetorian,
a private chess engine by Sander de Zoete which played the ICT 2003 and the ICT 2004. Praetorian's internal board representation relies on Reverse Bitboards [2] , which requires to serialize two sets for sliding piece move generation, one for the positive ray-directions and one for the reversed negative ray-directions.
