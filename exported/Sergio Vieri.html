Sergio Vieri [1]

Sergio Vieri,
an Indonesian computer scientist affiliated with the National University of Singapore and Google Singapore. At the International Olympiad in Informatics, he won Bronze and Silver in 2016 and 2017 respectively [2], and is now preparing and testing problems for selection and training for the Indonesian team [3]. Sergio Vieri is further successful chess trainer of Leela Chess. Relaxing the "Zero" paradigm of pure reinforcement learning by supervised learning techniques, he is famous for his strong networks [4]. More recently, his training tools and methods also gave Stockfish NNUE a kick with an increase in playing strength hardly thought possible before [5] [6].
