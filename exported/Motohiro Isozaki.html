Motohiro Isozaki, (やねうらお)
a Japanese Shogi programmer from Osaka. He is author of Yaneura King [2], and the 29th World Computer Shogi Champion [3] YaneuraOu [4] [5]. His YaneuraOu Official Website elaborates not only on YaneuraOu, but Shogi engines, and recently, the synergy of the Shogi community with the computer chess and in particular the Stockfish community, yielding in NNUE and Stockfish NNUE [6].
