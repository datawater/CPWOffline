Tom M. Mitchell,
an American computer scientist and AI researcher with focus on machine learning. He is Edward Fredkin University Professor and chair of the Machine Learning Department at Carnegie Mellon University [3], leading the development of the Never-Ending Language Learning (NELLL) system.
