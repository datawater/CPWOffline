Diep's withdrawal

Vincent Diepeveen was late Saturday morning for the upcoming Diep vs. IsiChess game of round 4. TD Jan Krabbenbos
had to start the clock after some maternity leave, and when Vincent arrived, sure that Diep would play 1. d4 anyway,
he already made the move by his own over the board, to start the game in time before his computer was ready. Jan
intervened, and Vincent flounced out of the room in a huff. Diep's withdrawal left an odd field for the remaining five rounds.

Winners

John van der Hoeven (Deep Sjeng), Hans van der Zijden and Jeroen Noomen (Rybka), Ernst Walet (Deep Shredder)
