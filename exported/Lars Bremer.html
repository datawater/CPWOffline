Lars Bremer,
a German software developer, journalist and writer with affinity to computer Games and computer chess. Lars was at times editor-in-chief for the German online magazine Computerschach und Spiele, wrote for the German computer magazine c't [1] and for Walter Eigenmann's online magazine Glarean [2]. On his own German site [3], one finds articles from Computerschach und Spiele and some interesting games like Lars' own Connect Four program Mustrum [4], as well as Chrilly's Corner [5].
