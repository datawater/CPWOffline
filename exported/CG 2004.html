Gonda Brain Research Center [1]

The Fourth International Conference on Computers and Games was held at the Gonda Brain Research Center of the Bar-Ilan University in Ramat Gan, Israel, July 5-7, 2004, in conjunction with the 9th Computer Olympiad and the 12th World Computer Chess Championship .
