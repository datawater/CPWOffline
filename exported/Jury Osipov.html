Jury Osipov [1]

Jury (Yuri) Osipov,
a Russian chess programmer, author of the chess program Strelka [2], which became second behind WildCat at the 1st Computer Chess Championship of CIS Countries in 2008. With Igor Korshunov co-author of the chess program Belka [3].
