Tobias Joppen [1]

Tobias Joppen,
a German computer scientist affiliated with TU Darmstadt and scientific staff in the knowledge engineering group headed by Johannes Fürnkranz [2]. His research interests include reinforcement learning, general game playing and Monte-Carlo tree search [3].
