chess-at-nite, (Chess At Nite)
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol compliant open source chess engine, by Franziskus Domig, Panayiotis Lipiridis, Radoslav Petrik and Thai Gia Tuong, written in C/C++, and released under the MIT license [1] .
