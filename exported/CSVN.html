History of the CSVN

The CSVN was founded in 1980 by ...

Max Euwe Jan Louwman Jaap van Oosterwijk Bruyn [1] Jaap van den Herik

... and others [2] . First chairman was Jaap van Oosterwijk Bruyn.


Tournaments

The CSVN yearly organizes the

  • Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship from 1981 to 2011 [3] , see Jan Louwman's announcement
  • International CSVN Tournament from 2001 to 2013
  • CSVN Programmers Tournament since 2013

as well as users tournaments with dedicated units.

Computerschaak

The CSVN regularly publishes the Computerschaak Journal, two issues a year. Editor is Hans van der Zijden, who in October 2010 superseded Eric van Reem.
