Jung-Kuei Yang,
a Taiwanese computer scientist at Lan Yang Institute of Technology, Toucheng, Yilan. He works and published on bitboard techniques and Monte-Carlo tree search applied to Connect6. He is primary author of the Connect6 program Kavalan, which won the 21st Computer Olympiad 2018.
