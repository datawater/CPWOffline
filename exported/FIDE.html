The Fédération Internationale des Échecs (FIDE) or World Chess Federation is an international organization that acts as the governing body of international chess competition, organizing the World Chess Championship, the Women's World Chess Championship, the World Junior and Girls U-20 Chess Championship, the World Senior Chess Championship [2], the World Youth Chess Championship, the Chess Olympiad, the World Team Chess Championship and regional championships. The FIDE was founded 1924 in Paris at the 1st unofficial Chess Olympiad as a kind of players' union by 15 participants and representatives of their countries.

FIDE and ICGA

The ICGA is recognized as Affiliated International Organization of the FIDE [6]. The WCCC 2006 along with the 37th Chess Olympiad in Turin was the first and so far the only time the ICGA was “sequentially assembled” by the FIDE [7].
