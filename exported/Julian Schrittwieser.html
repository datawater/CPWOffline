Julian Schrittwieser [1]

Julian Schrittwieser,
an Austrian computer scientist and senior software engineer at Google DeepMind. He is involved in the AlphaGo and AlphaZero projects mastering the games of Go, chess and Shogi, and holds a B.Sc. in software enginering from Vienna University of Technology in 2013 [2].
