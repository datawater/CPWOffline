Carlos Pesce,
an Argentinian chess player [1], computer chess expert and opening book author. Carlos worked with various chess program authors not only on their book, notably with Vincent Diepeveen on Diep [2], with Richard Pijl on The Baron [3], with Dieter Bürßner on Yace [4], with Tim Foden on Green Light Chess [5], with Gian-Carlo Pascutto on Deep Sjeng, with Sergei Markoff on SmarThink [6], with Igor Korshunov on WildCat [7], with Carmelo Calzerano on Leila, and with Miguel A. Ballicora on Gaviota [8], to name a few. Carlos was also involved in providing opening books for the Arena GUI.
