Leen Ammeraal [1]

Leen Ammeraal,
a Dutch mathematician and computer scientist, former lecturer at Utrecht University of Applied Sciences (Hogeschool Utrecht), and as computer chess programmer author of the chess engine Queen, and based on Queen, the Android app Senior Chess [2]. Leen is also author and co-author of programming books about C++, Java and Computer Graphics.
