Jan Kuipers,
a Dutch early computer chess programmer residing in Belgium [1]. He is author of Tiny Chess 86 (TC86) written in 8086 assembly, which played the Second PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship 1989 in London [2] and the First Dutch Computer Chess Championship 1981 in Utrecht.

Publications

  • Jan Kuipers (1981). Tiny Chess 86 - Een schaakprogramma voor de 8088/8086. Databus 06-81, pdf hosted by Hein Veldhuis
