Chun-Hung Tzeng [1]

Chun-Hung Tzeng, (Chunhung Tzeng)
a Taiwanese mathematician, computer scientist, and professor emeritus at Ball State University. He was professor of computer science at Ball State University and professor of mathematics at National Taiwan University, and holds a M.Sc. in mathematics from National Taiwan University in 1968, a Ph.D. in mathematics from University of Göttingen in 1973, and a Ph.D. in computer science from Indiana University Bloomington in 1983 [2]. His research interests include probabilistic reasoning and game tree search, minimax along with probabilistic game models, product propagation and pathology.
