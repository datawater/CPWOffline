MacChess [1]

MacChess,
a sophisticated and free chess program for Apple Macintosh computers developed by Wim van Beusekom. The chess engine was written in 68020 Assembly [2] and subsequent versions for the PowerPC [3]. MacChess competed the DOCCC 1995, the DOCCC 1996 and the Aegon 1997 Human-Machine tournament.
