Stéphane Nguyen [1]

Stéphane Nguyen,
a French computer chess programmer and author of the Java chess engine Jester [2]. Stéphane participated with Jester at multiple French Computer Chess Championships and French Programmers Tournaments, and maintained a Computer Chess Site, also reporting on the tournaments.
