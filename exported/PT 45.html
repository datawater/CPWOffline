The CSVN 45th Programmers Tournament, superseding the Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship and the International CSVN Tournament, took place at November 30 and December 01, 2013, Denksportcentrum Leiden.

Twelve programs participated, operated by their respective authors, including the brand new Deep Fritz by Gyula Horváth, and Shredder by Stefan Meyer-Kahlen. Jonny by Johannes Zwanzger, which played the WCCC 2013 on 2400 cores of the btrzx3 cluster of the University of Bayreuth [1] [2] , used "only" 216 cores but nevertheless won the tournament a half point ahead of its commercial rivals.
