The First Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship was held July 21-22, 2007. The format was Rapid Chess (G/28+1), played on-line over the internet (ICC). Bruce Wright, co-author of Duchess (2nd place WCCC 1977 & 3rd place WCCC 1980), served as guest commentator [1] .
