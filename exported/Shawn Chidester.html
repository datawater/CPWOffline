Shawn Chidester[1]

Shawn Chidester,
an American senior software engineer at Attensity [2], and as computer chess programmer author of the chess engines CACIO (early 90s in Turbo Pascal [3]), Clubfoot, its bitboard counterpart Bitfoot, the experimental Clunk [4], Bumblebee [5], and the Senjo C++ UCI adapter. Senjo simplifies the UCI protocol implementation of engines written in C++, as demonstrated by Clubfoot, Bitfoot and Bumblebee [6].
