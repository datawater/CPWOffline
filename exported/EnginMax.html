EnginMax,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol, and later UCI compliant chess engine by Engin Üstün, written in C and C++. The development started in 1995, a first release was published late 2000. An updated version played the CCT13 instead of its successor Tornado and gained a respectable 50% score.
