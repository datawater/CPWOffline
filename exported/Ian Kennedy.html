Ian Kennedy,
a British professional programmer holding a bachelor of science degree in astronomy from University College London [1]. He is author of the chess program Psycho, which played the UPCCC 1993 [2] and UPCCC 1994 Uniform-Platform Computer Chess Championship, and as of 2020 still under active development [3].
