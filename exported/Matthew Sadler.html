Matthew Sadler [1]

Matthew D. Sadler,
an English chess grandmaster, chess writer and two-time British Chess Champion, in 1995 and 1997 [2]. In 2017, he analyzed the computer games of AlphaZero versus Stockfish [3], and along with Natasha Regan co-authored the book Game Changer which details the lessons that can be learnt from how AlphaZero plays chess [4]. Since TCEC Season 14 in 2019, Matthew Sadler reports on the Superfinals of the Top Chess Engines Competition, published on TCEC and the ICGA Journal.
