Rotor [1]

Rotor, (ChessRotor)
an UCI compliant chess engine by Jan Brouwer, written in C++, which first appeared in rating lists in April 2007 [2] [3]. Rotor is available as Windows and Mac OS executable, as well as Android engine under Chess for Android [4].
