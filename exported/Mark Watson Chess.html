Mark Watson Chess, (Mark Watson)
an early chess program written in 1977/78 by Mark Watson in Basic for the Apple II, distributed by Apple on their demo cassette tape [1] [2]. Mark Watson Chess participated at the Second West Coast Computer Faire Microcomputer Chess Tournament in March 1978 in the class C section for Basic programs [3].
