Xinbo Gao [1]

Xinbo Gao,
a Chinese electrical engineer, computer scientist, and full professor and director of the Video and Image Processing System (VIPS) Laboratory, School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University, Xi'an, Shaanxi Province. He holds a Bachelor in electronic engineering, and Master and Ph.D. degrees in signal processing and information processing from Xidian University in 1994, 1997 and 1999, respectively. His research interests include visual information perception, pattern recognition, Fuzzy clustering, machine learning, computational intelligence [2], and applying deep learning with convolutional neural networks to facial recognition.

(D, d)-OM Search

In the late 90s, Xinbo Gao was research fellow in Hiroyuki Iida's group at Shizuoka University, Hamamatsu, Japan, where he worked along with Hiroyuki Iida, Jos Uiterwijk, and Jaap van den Herik on a generalization of opponent model search, called (D, d)-OM search, where D stands for the depth of search by the player and d for the opponent’s depth of search [3].
