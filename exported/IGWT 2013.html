The I.G.W.T. (International Gsei Web Tournament) is an international competition between chess engines relayed on gsei site [1] . In the first 2013 edition, the best 8 Italian engines, selected through a series of double round robin, versus 8 non Italian engines, selected through a one double round robin (10 participants). After that, this 16 engines played a knockout, the final step, with Chiron as winner [2] .
