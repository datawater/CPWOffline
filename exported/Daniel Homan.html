Danien Homan [1]

Daniel C. Homan,
an American astrophysicist and associate professor at Denison University, Granville, Ohio. He received his Ph.D. in physics from Brandeis University in 1999. As avocational computer chess programmer Daniel Homan is author of the experimental Chess Engine Communication Protocol compatible chess engine EXchess [2].
