Bruno Serge Pascal Bras,
a French computer chess and Othello programmer, and owner of the publishing company EBBIZ, distributing his software [1]. He is author of the chess programs Nest [2] , Gemie, Pacque Expert [3] , Chess Christy [4], Kristy Lenix (with co-author M. Descloitre, written in Assembly [5] ), Soft Jacqueline Bras [6], and most recently, Chess Ebbiz 9 written in x86 Assembly, which was plagued by some bugs during the WCSC 2017.
