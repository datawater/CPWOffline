Revenge,
an UCI compliant chess engine by Fabio Gobbato, released in July 2021 as commercial successor of Pedone 3.1 but with many improvements [2]. Revenge is available for Windows x86-64, Linux x86-64, and Linux ARMv7 and ARMv8.

Features

[3]

  • Pure Neural Network Evaluation (Improved from Pedone)
  • Chess960
  • MultiPV for Analysis
  • Parallel Search
    • Reverse Futility Pruning (Tuned)
    • History Reductions (New)
    • ProbCut (Improved)
  • Skill Levels (Improved)
  • Large/Huge Pages
  • Internal 4men Tablebases
  • Syzygy Bases Support
  • Polyglot Book Support
