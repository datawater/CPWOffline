Roberto Munter [1]

Roberto Munter,
an Italien industrial consultant and computer chess programmer. He is author of the Ippolit and IvanHoe derivatives Deep Saros, Elektro [2] [3] and OldSnake [4], and primary author of Vitruvius [1].
