Dorpsgek,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol compliant chess engine by Matthew R. Brades, written in C, previously available as open source licensed under the GNU General Public License [3]. Dorpsgek versions were named after Cocktails in alphabetic order, starting with Ambrosia released in July 2016 [4].
