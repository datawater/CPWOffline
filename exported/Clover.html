Clover [1]

Clover,
an UCI compliant open source chess engine by Luca Metehau, written in C++. Clover was first released in April 2021 [2], hosted on GitHub. In January 2022, Clover 3.0 was released featuring NNUE instead of hand crafted evaluation [3].
