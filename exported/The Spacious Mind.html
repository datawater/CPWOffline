Spacious Mind,
pseudonym of Nick Milicev [1], a computer chess aficionado and collector of dedicated chess computers as well as chess programs for home- and personal computers. He maintains The Spacious Mind sites [2] with a lot of informations about chess computers and programs, referred from multiple CPW sites on these topics.
