Franck Zibi [1]

Franck Zibi,
a French computer scientist, chess player, and senior developer at Societe Generale Corporate and Investment Banking [2]. As computer chess programmer, he is author of the chess programs HpChess for the HP48 calculators family written in C, and its successors, the WinBoard compatible engines ZChess and Pharaon, both written in C++, the latter also UCI conform.
