IEEE,
the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, a professional association headquartered in New York City, dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence. IEEE produces 30% of the world's literature in the electrical and electronics engineering and computer science fields, publishing well over 100 peer-reviewed journals [1].
