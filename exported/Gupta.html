Subodh Gupta - Line of Control [1]

Gupta,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol compatible, cross-platform, didactic open source chess engine by Jelle Geerts, written in C++. Gupta maintains a 0x88 board and applies plain negamax alpha-beta without iterative deepening and transposition table so far.
