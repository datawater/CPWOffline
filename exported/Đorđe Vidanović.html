Đorđe Vidanović [1]

Đorđe Vidanović,
a Serbian professor of semantics and cognitive science at the University of Niš. Đorđe Vidanović is a computer chess expert, early supporter of the Chess Engine Communication Protocol protocol and WinBoard Engines, and the opening book author of various chess engines, beside others, notably for Ruffian and early Rybka versions [2] [3] , and along with primary author Vladan Vučković co-author of chess engine Axon, and also involved in its parallel incarnation Achilles, responsible for the book moves [4] .
