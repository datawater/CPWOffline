Chao-Chin Liang,
a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Computer Games and Intelligence Lab at National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu headed by professor I-Chen Wu [1]. His research interests include machine learning for computer games and mathematical optimization [2]. Along with Kun-Hao Yeh and I-Chen Wu, Chao-Chin Liang is co-author of the 2048 bot CGI-2048 [3].
