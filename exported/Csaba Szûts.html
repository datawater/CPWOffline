Csaba Szûts [1]

Csaba Szûts,
a Hungarian chess player and opening book author of the chess programs Pandix by primary author Gyula Horváth [2], also involved in the commercial release of Pandix ChessFriend [3], and more recently, Crux by primary author László Szalai [4].
