Logic Crazy [1]

Jonathan Warkentin, (Logic Crazy)
a Canadian programmer who has been fascinated with the challenge of not only writing chess engines, but also helping others achieve the same feat. He provides tutorials ranging from simplistic engines to advanced engines in video series format. His tutorials demonstrate and explain every step necessary to creating the engine at hand. Check out his videos on his YouTube Channel [2].
