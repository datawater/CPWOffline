Peter Skinner [1]

Peter Skinner,
a Canadian information technology and services professional, web developer, computer chess aficionado and tester, since CCT7 in 2005 until its last appearance, the CCT16 in 2014, organizer of the CCT Tournaments [2]. Peter Skinner was member of the Crafty team around Robert Hyatt [3].
