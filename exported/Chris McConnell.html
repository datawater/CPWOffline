Christopher C. McConnell,
an American computer scientist and Microsoft employee, alumnus from the Carnegie Mellon University. At CMU as P.D. Student, Chris McConnell worked with Berliner on the chess program HiTech [1], and was involved in researching the B* probability based search and in tuning evaluation.
