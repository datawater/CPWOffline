Sacha Droste,
a German computer scientist and software architect. As former member of the Knowledge Engineering Group headed by Johannes Fürnkranz at TU Darmstadt, he defended his master thesis on supervised reinforcement learning and temporal difference learning in chess and the chess variants Crazyhouse, Suicide Chess and Atomic Chess, using Sunsetter by Georg von Zimmermann as test-bed of his experiments [1]. The matter is to learn piece values and piece-square values.
