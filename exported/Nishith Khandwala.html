Nishith Khandwala [1]

Nishith Khandwala,
an Indian American computer scientist in the AI in healthcare industry, and co-founder of the Y Combinator launched startup Bunkerhill Health [2], founded in 2019, based in Palo Alto, California. Before, he was affiliated with the Center for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine & Imaging, Stanford University [3], where he already obtained his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in artificial intelligence [1]. Along with Barak Oshri at Stanford in 2015, he worked on a convolutional neural network project to predict moves in Chess, dubbed ConvChess [4] [5].
