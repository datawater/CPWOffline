Leo Dijksman, (died on February 18, 2015)
was a Dutch computer chess expert, tester and promoter of WinBoard engines, and organizer of one of the highest quality engine competitions ever for many years, WBEC Ridderkerk or WinBoard Engine Competition [2], also hosting and maintaining the associated chessengine news and tournament site [3]. Leo Dijksman died in February 2015 at age 61 after battling cancer for almost two years [4] [5].
