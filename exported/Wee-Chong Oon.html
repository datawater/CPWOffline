Wee-Chong Oon [1]

Wee-Chong Oon, (Wee Chong Oon)
a Singaporean computer scientist and lecturer at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. He holds a Ph.D. in computer science from National University of Singapore in 2007, and was postdoctoral researcher at City University of Hong Kong until 2011 [2]. His research interests include data structures and algorithms, operations research, artificial intelligence, machine learning and interactive and abstract games like Kalah and Backgammon.
