Home * Software * Web Broadcast

Web Broadcast systems transmit live games from tournaments or matches on the internet. Beside proprietary commercial systems transmitting games from human chess tournaments or chess servers, there are solutions to broadcast engine-engine matches played under control of a tournament manager of a CLI or GUI, watching them world-wide in a web browser or dedicated game viewer application.
