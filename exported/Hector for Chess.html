Hector [1]

Hector for Chess, (HfC)
a general game playing search core module (Hector) along with a compile time connected game specific chess module, both written by Csaba Jergler in C++, supporting both, the Chess Engine Communication Protocol and the Universal Chess Interface [2].

Hector for Chess played the WCCC 2010 and WCSC 2010 ICGA World Computer Chess Championship and World Chess Software Championship in Kanazawa [3], four ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championships, the WCRCC 2007, WCRCC 2008, WCRCC 2009, and WCRCC 2010, the CCT11, and the MASPV 2007 and MASPV 2008 Hungarian Chessprogram Open. Its predecessor under the name Hector was written in Delphi, and competed at the MASPV 2001 and MASPV 2002 [4], where it had tough fights versus the Pandora.
