The IGWT IV, the International Gsei Web Tournament IV edition, was held in February and March 2017. Eight Italian engines played the final steps versus eight guest engines - both groups required qualification tournaments. Again, Andscacs by Daniel José Queraltó and Chiron by Ubaldo Andrea Farina won their matches in the round of sixteen, the quarterfinal and semifinal to play the hard-fought final. This edition, Andscacs could take revenge from last years defeat, and became the lucky but deserved winner [1] [2].
