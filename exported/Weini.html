Weini,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol compliant open source chess engine by Vivien Clauzon. The project started in December 2016, and became a kind of interactive forum development when its author asked for advice one year later [1]. Many features were implemented and tuned over the time, many techniqes were tried, possibly starting suffering maintainability, and desire for a re-write [2]. Weini is an acronym for WisEness Is Not Inside [3].
