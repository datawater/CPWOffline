Xinhe Xu, (Xin-He Xu)
a Chinese computer scientist and professor at School of Information Science and Engineering, Northeastern University China, Shenyang, and previously at Shenyang Institute of Chemical Technology, College of Information Engineering, Shenyang. His research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, robot control, computer vision and computer games. He is author of the chinese chess program MonkeyKing, and co-author the Connect6 program NEU6Star. Xinhe Xu is Section Editor of the ICGA Journal, and was involved in organizing the 13th Computer Olympiad, including the WCCC 2008 and CG 2008 conference in Beijing.
