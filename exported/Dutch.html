Dutch,
the Delft University of Technology Chess Program developed by Sito Dekker, Harry Nefkens, Roger Hünen, Jaap van den Herik and Tom Pronk [1]. Like its forerunner Pion, Dutch was written in C. It participated at the WCCC 1986 in Cologne with 3 out of 5 [2], and further played the DOCCC 1986 and DOCCC 1987.
