Futé,
a private chess program written by Jean-Louis Boussin. Futé participated at the WCCC 2005 in Reykjavík and scored 0.5/11, but was the one and only program to take even a half point off of winner Zappa. Futé applied the Plan of Attack, a pre-processing technique performed at the root to control its search and evaluation [1].
