Sabrina,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol chess engine by Stefano Gemma, written in C++, first released in June 2017 as purified Satana [2]. Sabrina is available to play under 32-bit and 64-bit Windows, Linux and Mac OSX [3], and was active at the IGT 2017 [4].
