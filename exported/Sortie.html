Sortie de Métro de Paris [1]

Sortie,
a chess program by Stephen F. Becker from Bucknell University [2]. Sortie played the ACM 1975, becoming unlucky last with one draw in round 3 versus Iron Fish, losing from later winner Chess 4.4, Ostrich and Tyro.
