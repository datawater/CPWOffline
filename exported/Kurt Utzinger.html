Kurt Utzinger [1]

Kurt Utzinger,
a Swiss retired primary school teacher, chess player, president of the chess club Pfäffikon ZH [2] [3], and computer chess aficionado and tester. Kurt Utzinger maintaines the computer chess site Kurt & Rolf Chess, full of (annotated) pgn files, test-positions, engines and utilities [4]. He is further active in various computer chess forums.
