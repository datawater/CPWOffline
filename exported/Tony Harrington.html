Anthony (Tony) Harrington,
a British financial, pensions, and business journalist. His areas of expertise include corporate finance, investment strategy, global payment systems, banking, property, hedge fund activities, charities, social housing, CSR reporting, environment, intellectual property, IT [1], and in the 80s computer chess.
