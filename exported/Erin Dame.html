Erin Dame [1]

Erin Dame,
an American chess player [2], and software and web developer affiliated with the Research Computing Center, University of New Hampshire, where he already received his BSc. degree in computer science in 1996. Combining his professional and avocational interests, he is working on his own chess engine dubbed RookieMonster since May 2016 [3].
