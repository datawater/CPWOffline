FIBChess,
a Java chess program by Guillermo Baches García [1], during the development affiliated with Barcelona School of Informatics (Facultat d'Informàtica de Barcelona, FIB) of Polytechnic University of Catalonia, Barcelona, Spain. FIBChess played the WCCC 2004 in Ramat Gan operated by Alejandro González Romero, and the WCCC 2006 in Turin operated by its author himself.
