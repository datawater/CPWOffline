Paolo Ciancarini [1]

Paolo Ciancarini,
an Italian computer scientist and professor at the University of Bologna. His research interests covers distributed search, computer chess and KriegSpiel. Along with Gian Piero Favini, Paolo Ciancarini is co-author of the KriegSpiel program and two times Gold Medal winner Darkboard, written in Java [2] .
