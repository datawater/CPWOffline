YNCT, (You Never Can Tell)
a chess program by Luuk de Vries, written in Z80 assembly or even Machine Code to ran on a Sharp MZ-80 [1]. YNCT 1.0 won the First Dutch Computer Chess Championship 1981, and YNCT 2.0 was runner-up at the DOCCC 1982.
