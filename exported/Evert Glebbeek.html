Evert Glebbeek [1]

Evert Glebbeek,
a Dutch astrophysicist at Radboud Universiteit Nijmegen [2] and before at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. As avocational games and chess programmer, Evert Glebbeek is author of the open source engines Jazz, Sjaak and Leonidas, all under the terms of the GNU General Public Licence. Sjaak and Leonidas are able to play various chess variants [3], Sjaak not to confused with Ronald de Man's engine with the same name [4].
