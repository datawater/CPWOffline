Truong-Huy D. Nguyen [1]

Truong-Huy Dinh Nguyen,
a Vietnamese computer scientist and software engineer at Google, Ph.D. from Singapore Management University in 2013. His research lies at the cross-junction of various research fields, including data analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and behavior analytics, with favorite applications being games and robotics [2]. Along with Wee Sun Lee et al., he researched and published on Monte-Carlo Tree Search and UCT variant called UCT-Aux, an enhancement technique with a heuristic policy to speed up the selection of optimal actions, which augments the look-ahead tree with auxiliary arms that are evaluated by the heuristic policy [3].
