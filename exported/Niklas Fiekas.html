Niklas Fiekas,
a German chess player and aficionado, open source software developer [1], and computer scientist affiliated with Clausthal University of Technology [2] . He is primary author of python-chess, a chess library written in Python featuring move generation and validation, PGN reading [3] and opening book reading.
