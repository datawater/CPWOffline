Ziggy Stardust [1]

Ziggy,
a chess program for the Commodore 64 by Henk Kuyer and Auke Punter, written in Pascal and 6502 assembly [2]. Ziggy played the DOCCC 1985, DOCCC 1986 and DOCCC 1987, as well as three early Aegon Tournaments.
