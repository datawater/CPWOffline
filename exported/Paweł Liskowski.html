Paweł Liskowski [1]

Paweł Liskowski,
a Polish computer scientist and assistant professor at the Institute of Computing Science, Poznań University of Technology. His research interest include various subfields of artificial intelligence, such as evolutionary computation, genetic programming, deep learning, reinforcement learning, machine learning and pattern recognition [2]. His 2012 master's thesis focuses on comparison and analysis of methods that learn the Othello position evaluation [3].
