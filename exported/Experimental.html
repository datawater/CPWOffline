Experimental,
a chess program by Wolfgang Delmare and Thorsten Kohfeldt. It was an experimental successor of Check Check, written in C running on a PC under MS-DOS. Experimental participated at the IPCCC 1993 and short later, at the WMCCC 1993 in Munich [2].
