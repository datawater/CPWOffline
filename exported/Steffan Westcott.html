Steffan Westcott [1]

Steffan Westcott,
an English senior software engineer affiliated with the travel management company Click Travel Ltd. [2], focusing on frameworks for enterprise applications on Amazon Web Services (AWS) using domain-driven design (DDD) [3]. He holds a BA degree in mathematics and computation from Keble College, Oxford in 1993, and brought many innovations into chess programming concerning bitboards.
