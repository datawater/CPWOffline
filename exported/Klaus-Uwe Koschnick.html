Klaus-Uwe Koschnick [1]

Klaus-Uwe Koschnick,
a German mahematician, computer scientist, and managing director of a consulting company with expertise in the airline and logistics industry, where he is responsible for new product development, operations research and project management. He holds a diplom and Ph.D. in mathematics from Bielefeld University in 1990, where his advisors were Rudolf Ahlswede and Hans-Georg Carstens. Klaus-Uwe Koschnick is author of the Backgammon playing program Bax, Gold medal winner of the 4th Computer Olympiad, London 1992 [2].
