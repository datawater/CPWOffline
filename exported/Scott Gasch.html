Scott Gasch [1]

Scott Gasch,
an American computer scientist, and software engineer at Google Inc. since 2005, before affiliated with Microsoft. He holds a B.Sc. in computer science from Virginia Tech. Scott is author of the open source chess programs Monsoon and its multithreaded successor Typhoon [2].
