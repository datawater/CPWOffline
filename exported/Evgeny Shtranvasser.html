Evgeny Shtranvasser [1]

Evgeny Shtranvasser,
an Israeli software engineer and Linux DevOps at VMware, previously affiliated with Intel Israel. As computer chess programmer, Evgeny Shtranvasser is author of the UCI compliant open source chess engine Bismark [2], and co-author of Chess Blunders, a chess database of games and puzzles [3].
