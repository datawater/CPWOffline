TCEC Season 7,
a private tournament directed and organized by Martin Thoresen and supported by the Chessdom Arena, started on September 19, 2014. There are 4 Stages plus the Superfinal [2]. All book openings until Stage 4 were selected by Nelson Hernandez alias Cato the Younger and Adam Hair, except in Stage 2 which will be run without books. The 32 openings of the Superfinal were selected by IM Erik Kislik. Komodo 1333 won the Superfinal versus Stockfish 141214 with 33½-30½.
