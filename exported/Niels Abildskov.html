Niels Abildskov [1]

Niels Abildskov,
a Danish computer scientits with interests in computer chess programming. He is author of the UCI compliant open source chess engines Copper [2] and Loki. Copper, his first chess engine trial was heavily influenced by Vice, is actually buggy and superseded by Loki [3].
