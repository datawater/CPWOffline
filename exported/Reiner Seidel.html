Reiner Seidel [1]

Reiner Seidel, (Rainer Seidel [2] [3])
a German psychologist, associate professor at Free University of Berlin [4], chess player and chess theorist who coined a scientific Chess theory as introduced in his 1987 book Grundlagen einer wissenschaftlichen Schachtheorie [5], also topic of two Advances in Computer Chess Conferences and various ICCA Journal articles.
