Netherlands-vs-Computers-1989,
a match between the 12 human chess masters qualified for the Dutch National Championship and strong computers took place in Hilversum, April 9, 1989, sponsored by Philips and AVRO [1] . Time control was 1h/60 moves + 1h and then adjudication if the game is not finished. The Dutch team won 8½ - 3½!
