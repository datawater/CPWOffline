Matheus,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol and UCI compatible chess engine by Josué Forte, written in C. Matheus had already its debut at Don Beal's Uniform-Platform CCC 1994 [1], and was first published as WinBoard engine in 2002 [2]. Matheus applies a principal variation search using a two-tier replacement scheme transposition table [3].
