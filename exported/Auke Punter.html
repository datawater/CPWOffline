Auke Punter,
a Dutch chess player, during the mid 80s successful newcomer of the Delftsche Schaakclub [1], as well as involved in computer chess programming. Along with Henk Kuyer, he developed the chess program Ziggy for the Commodore 64, which played the DOCCC 1985, DOCCC 1986 and DOCCC 1987 [2].
