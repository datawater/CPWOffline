TCEC Season 4,
a tournament directed and organized by Martin Thoresen, held from January 22 until April 28, 2013. After the break in 2011, the TCEC Season 4 was initially relaunched as nTCEC Season 1 [2]. Houdini 3 won the Superfinal versus Stockfish 250413 with 25 - 23.
