Salvador Pallares [1]

Salvador Pallares Bejarano,
a Spanish entrepreneur, business and technology consultant, who holds a Bachelor degree in business studies from University of Seville in 1997 [2]. As a chess player [3] and programmer, he started chess programming in 2001, developing his first private engine Littleclara, superseded by his Chess Engine Communication Protocol and UCI compatible chess engine Clarabit [4], which started to play tournaments in 2007, and was published in 2008 [5].
