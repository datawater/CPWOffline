Luca Naddei,
an Italian software developer and computer games and chess programmer. He is author of the chess engine and GUI Uragano 3D [1], which competed at various Italian Computer Chess Championships and the WCCC 2006 in Turin [2], and the chess engine PICcolino, intended to run on a PIC microcontroller as part of a self build dedicated chess computer. PICcolino had its over the board debut at the IGT 2017 and played the IGT 2018 already as dedicated unit..
