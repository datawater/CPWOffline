Max Bramer [1]

Max A. Bramer,
a British computer scientist and emeritus professor of Information Technology at the University of Portsmouth. Former appointments include lecturer in Computer Science at the Open University, Milton Keynes. His research interests include Data mining and formerly the application of declarative knowledge of chess endgames with a small number of pieces like KPK.
