Christian Barreteau [1]

Christian Barreteau,
a French electrical engineer with a Diplôme universitaire de technologie (DUT) degree from University of Angers in 1983. As computer chess programmer, Christian Barreteau is author of the chess engine AnMon. He started chess programming end of the 80s, and made AnMon a MTD(f) searcher in about 1995.
