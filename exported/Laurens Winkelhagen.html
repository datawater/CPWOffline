Laurens Winkelhagen [1]

Laurens Winkelhagen,
a Dutch computer scientist and senior software engineer. He holds a M.Sc. in artificial intelligence from Utrecht University in 2006. As computer chess programmer, Laurens Winkelhagen is author of the chess engines JanWillem, released in 2004, and FrankWalter, first released in 2009 [2], revised in 2018 [3], and published as open source in February 2019 [4].
