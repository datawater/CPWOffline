Queen Mary, University of London,
London, United Kingdom, a constituent college of the federal University of London with roots dating back to 1785 [2]. In 1989 Queen Mary College merged with Westfield College to form Queen Mary & Westfield College. In 2000, the college adopted its present title of Queen Mary, University of London.
