The Venetian Macau [1]

8th World Chess Software Championship 2019,
on August 11 and 12, 2019, in The Venetian Macau, Macau, China in conjunction with the WCCC 2019, the 22nd Computer Olympiad and the Advances in Computer Games 16 conference, all organized by the ICGA, alongside with the 28th IJCAI conference [2]. Six participants played a double round robin with a time control of 45 minutes per side plus 15 seconds increment per move. Common hardware was a Levono Windos 10 system with a Pentium Silver x86-64 processor at 1.1 GHz [3]. Komodo was able to defend its title.
