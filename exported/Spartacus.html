Spartacus' death [1]

Spartacus,
a chess engine by Harm Geert Muller based on an internal 24x10 board (half of it usable, like the 16x8 board of 0x88) to easier implement 10x8 or 10x10 chess variants. The idea was to base the move generator on an incrementally updated attack map, so that it would naturally generate in MVV-LVA order [2]. Spartacus played the ICT 2011, CPT 2011, DOCCC 2011, and further International CSVN and subsequent CSVN Programmers Tournaments.
