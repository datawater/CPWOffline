Siberian Chess,
a DOS shareware chess program by Eugene Nalimov. Siberian Chess was developed in the 80's and early 90s, and was mainly written in C with time-critical parts in 8080 and later 8086 assembly [1]. It was distributed by Gambit-Soft around 1994.
