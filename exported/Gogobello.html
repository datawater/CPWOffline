Gogobello,
a free UCI compliant chess engine by Salvatore Giannotti, written in C and first released in November 2014 as WinBoard engine. Gogobello had its tournament debut at IGT 2015, when it became sixth from nine - and further improved at the IGT 2016 as fourth from ten with 4Â½/7.
