Bo-Nian Chen,
a Taiwanese computer scientist at Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University. His research includes knowledge abstraction and inference in the game domains of Computer Chinese Chess [1] and their Endgame Tablebases, and Chinese Dark Chess or Half Chess [2], a two players board game, played on a 4x8 grid. Along with Tsan-sheng Hsu, Bo-Nian Chen is co-author of the Chinese Dark Chess program Flipper.
