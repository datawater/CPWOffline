Severi Salminen [1]

Severi Salminen,
a Finnish professional violinist, master of music from Sibelius Academy, Helsinki, computer scientist with B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Aalto University in 2016 and 2019 respectively [2], and software developer and former computer chess programmer. He is author of ssRay, an unbiased Monte Carlo path tracing renderer capable of producing very realistic looking images [3], and author of the WinBoard compliant chess engine Requiem [4].
