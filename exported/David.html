David [1]

David,
an early chess program by Gerhard Wolf [2], in the 70s affiliated with the Graz University of Technology and its computer center Rechenzentrum Graz, Austria. David played the ACM 1971 in Chicago and ran on an UNIVAC 494 at UNIVAC Division, St. Paul, Minnesota, taking 10K 30-bit words of memory [3]. David lost from Tech, won versus Schach and drew CCCP.
