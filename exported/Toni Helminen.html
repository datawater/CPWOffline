Toni Helminen, (JohnWoe)
a Finnish IT freelancer, and computer games and computer chess programmer. He is author of multiple open source chess and chess variant engines [1], the Shogi engine RubyShogi, the chess variant engine Shuriken, the Gothic Chess engine Havoc, and the chess playing engines Sapeli and Mayhem, the latter featuring NNUE [2].
