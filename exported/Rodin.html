Auguste Rodin [1]

Rodin,
a WinBoard compliant chess engine by Fermin Serrano, written in C using Code::Blocks with MinGW. First released in August 2008, it initially used Pradu Kannan's Magic Bitboard move generator [2]. Rodin v8.0. has its own magics.
