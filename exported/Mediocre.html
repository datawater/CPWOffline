Mediocre

Mediocre, (Mediocre Chess)
an UCI compliant chess engine by Jonatan Pettersson, written entirely in Java. Mediocre is the result of a chess programming blog, which describes aspects of the engine in detail [1]. Mediocre played the WCRCC 2007 and gained 7Â½ out of 14.
