Fauzi Akram [1]

Fauzi Akram Dabat,
a Jordanian chess player, computer chess aficionado and opening book author. In 2004, he started to create opening books for different GUI and formats, and now prefers the abk-Format of the Arena GUI [2]. In August 2015, Stockfish 6 by Marco Costalba, Joona Kiiski and Tord Romstad topped of the SSDF ratinglist using the "Aggressive 1.3 by Fauzi.abk" opening book by Fauzi Akram Dabat [3] [4].
