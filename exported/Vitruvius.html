Vitruvian Man [1]

Vitruvius, (VitruviusChess)
a commercial, UCI compatible chess engine built on the skeleton structure of the free Ippolit programs, that is using IvanHoe and its derivative Deep Saros as code base [2], with a speculative evaluation and playing style. The Vitruvius team consists of programmer Roberto Munter, book author Andrea Manzo and principal tester Domenico Lattanzi [3]. Vitruvius won the 3rd Italian Open Chess Software Cup 2012 [4].
