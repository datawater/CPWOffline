Porta Nigra in Trier, Frank's hometown [1]

FCT1, a rating list maintained and hosted by Frank Quisinsky, considering UCI and Winboard engines running under Windows 7 Professional SP1 x64, 45 minutes per side and game [2]. FCT1 started in May 2014 [3].
