TCEC Season 1,
the first TCEC season directed and organized by Martin Thoresen, held from December 04, 2010 until February 06, 2011. Houdini 1.5a won the nTCEC Season 1 Superfinal with a margin of 23½-16½ versus Rybka 4.0.
