Raptor,
an experimental chess program by Frédéric Louguet, which played the Massy 2003 French Programmers Tournament, where it became runner-up behind Chess Tiger in a field of 18 programs. Frédéric Louguet explicitly stated that Raptor is definitely not Chess Wizard, but a different and experimental program [2].
