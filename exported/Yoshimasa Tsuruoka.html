Yoshimasa Tsuruoka [1]

Yoshimasa Tsuruoka,
a Japanese computer scientist, Shogi programmer and associate professor at University of Tokyo and before at School of Information Science [2] , Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST). His research topics include natural language processing, text mining from biomedical literature, artificial intelligence in games, and deep learning. Yoshimasa Tsuruoka is co-author of the Shogi engine Gekisashi, Gold medal winner of the 15th Computer Olympiad, Shogi in Kanazawa.
