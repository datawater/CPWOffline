Being Demolished [1]

Demolito,
an UCI compliant open source chess engine by Lucas Braesch, licensed under the GNU General Public License, written in C, first announced at OpenChess in January 2017 [2], a revised stable release published in June 2017 as completely rewritten new DiscoCheck featuring SMP and Chess960 [3], with executables available for Linux, Mac OS and Android.
