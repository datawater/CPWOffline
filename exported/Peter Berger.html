Peter Berger [1]

Peter Berger,
a German computer chess expert, chess player and opening book author of various programs, most notable the Bringer by Gerrit Reubold and Crafty by Robert Hyatt. Peter represented and operated Crafty at three consecutive World Computer Chess Championships, the WCCC 2004 in Ramat Gan, WCCC 2005 in Reykjavik and WCCC 2006 in Turin.
