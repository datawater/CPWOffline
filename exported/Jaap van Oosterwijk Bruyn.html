Jaap (J.J.) van Oosterwijk Bruyn (February 01, 1924 - July 2003),
was a Dutch mathematician, actuarial scientist, and gerontologists. He was a master chess player [2] , and in the 80s the first chairman of the Dutch Computer Chess Federation, as well of the 5th Advances in Computer Chess Comference [3]. He also wrote various reports on tournaments and matches in Computerschaak [4] .
