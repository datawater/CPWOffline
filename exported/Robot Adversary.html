Robot Adversary [1] [2]

Robot Adversary, (Novag Robot Adversary)
Novag's 1982 release of a dedicated chess computer with a mechatronical actuator, a Robotic arm making its own moves, and a magnetic sensory board to recognize moves made by the player. Robot Adversary had a 32 KiB Z80 program by David Kittinger based on MyChess, similar to the program as used in the Savant and Savant II [3].
