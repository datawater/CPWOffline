Karsten Bauermeister,
a German attorney-at-law [3], computer chess expert, collector of dedicated chess computers [4], computer chess journalist, tester and operator. He wrote for Computerschach und Spiele and wrote and published a history of computer chess [5]. Karsten operated HIARCS at the WMCCC 1995 in Paderborn, Diogenes at WMCCC 1996 in Jakarta and Comet at the WMCCC 1997 in Paris .
