Delta,
a microcomputer chess program by Dave Wilson which participated at the PCW-MCC 1979 as part of the Personal Computer World exhibition, London [3]. Wilson later joint forces with Mike Johnson, and Delta and Mike were incorporated into their bit sliced system Advance [4].
