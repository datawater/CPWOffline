Barak Oshri [1]

Barak Oshri,
an Israeli computer scientist and machine learning engineer at Sius, a digital design agency based in Los Angeles. He holds an IB diploma from United World College of South East Asia, Singapore in 2013, a B.Sc. degree in mathematics and a M.Sc. in artificial intelligence and machine learning from Stanford University in 2017 and 2018 respectively [1]. Along with Nishith Khandwala at Stanford in 2015, he worked on a convolutional neural network project to predict moves in Chess, dubbed ConvChess [2] [3].
