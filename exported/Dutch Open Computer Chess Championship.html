Home * Tournaments * Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship

From 1981 until 2011 the Dutch Computer Championship was held, organized by the CSVN, since 1994 the Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship. Along with the International CSVN Tournament, the DOCCC was continued in 2013 under the name CSVN Programmers Tournament.
