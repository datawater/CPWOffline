Jiao Wang [1]

Jiao Wang,
a Chinese computer scientist affiliated with the School of Information Science and Engineering, Northeastern University China, Shenyang. He is author of the Chinese Chess program NEUChess, Bronce medal winner of the 10th Computer Olympiad, Taipei 2005 and two times Gold medal winner, at the 11th Computer Olympiad, Turin 2006 [2] and the 12th Computer Olympiad, Amsterdam 2007.
