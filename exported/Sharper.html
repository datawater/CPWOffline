Card-Sharpers [1]

Sharper,
a Chess Engine Communication Protocol aka WinBoard compliant chess engine by Albert Bertilsson, written in C++. Sharper emerged in 2002 and 2003 from Albert's former engine #Chess, which was written in C# [2]. Released in 2003 [3], Sharper became a reference engine for Perft and Divide results.
