Zacharias Georgiou [1]

Zacharias Georgiou,
a Greek Cypriot computer scientist and Ph.D. student at University of Cyprus, where he already received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees, researching in the area of data analytics in fog computing [2]. As exchange student at the University of Groningen, he worked on chess - the project work along with Evangelos Karountzos, Yaroslav Shkarupa and Matthia Sabatelli dealt with reinforcement learning in simple chess endgames such as KRK [3].
