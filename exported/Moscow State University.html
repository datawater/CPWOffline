Lomonosov Moscow State University,
Moscow, Russia, previously known as Lomonosov University or MSU (университет Ломоносова, Universitét Lomonósova; МГУ, MGU), is the oldest and largest university in Russia, founded in 1755, the university was renamed in honor of its founder, Mikhail Lomonosov, in 1940 [2].
