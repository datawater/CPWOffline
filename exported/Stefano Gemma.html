Stefano Gemma at CIPS 2001 [1]

Stefano Gemma,
an Italian IT consultant, owner of L'Informatica di Gemma Stefano [2], and as computer chess programmer, founding member of the Gruppo Scacchi e Informatica, the Italian Computer Chess Association, and author of the chess programs Drago, Raffaela [3], Freccia, Satana [4], and Sabrina [5].
