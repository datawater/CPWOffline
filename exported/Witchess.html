Witchess, (PMess, Npmess, NPMchess)
a private chess engine by Mridul Muralidharan, presumably successor or branch of his experimental MessChess program. Witchess played a strong CCT7, the CCT8 under the handle PMess (parallel Mess) [2], and the CCT9 as NPMchess [3]. At ICC, Witchess is known under the handle Npmess, applying a parallel search on a dual core AMD processor [4].
