Johanna Hellemons [1]

Johanna (Joke) Hellemons,
a Dutch scientist, scientific colleague of Jaap van den Herik, and representative of the ICGA - until August 2008 at the Maastricht University, then at the Tilburg Centre for Creative Computing (TiCC) of the Tilburg University [2], and since 2014 at Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science (LIACS), Leiden University, where Johanna Hellemons will continue her work as Editorial Manager of the ICGA Journal [3].
