Chua Kong Sian (Kong Sian),
a Singaporean computer scientist affiliated with the National University of Singapore and its former National Supercomputing Research Centre Singapore [1]. As computer chess programmer, Chua Kong Sian is author of Cobalt, primary author of GNU Chess Version 5 [2], and author of the C++ UCI bitboard-based engine Melee Chess [3].
