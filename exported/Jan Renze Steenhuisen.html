Renze Steenhuisen [1]

Jan Renze Steenhuisen,
a Dutch senior software engineer and computer scientist with interests in computer chess, computer games, and multi-agent planning. He holds M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees from TU Delft in 2005 and 2013 respectively. His 2005 Master's thesis was about Parallel Search, the design and implementation of a parallel two-player state space searcher called DarkSight [2], performing Transposition-driven scheduling [3] [4].
