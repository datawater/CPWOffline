Matacz,
a free chess engine with Chess Engine Communication Protocol aka WinBoard support, developed by Maciej Pestka since 2002. Matacz became Champion of Poland in 2004 (uniform category), in 2006 (uniform category) and won the award of the best polish chess engine in 2007 and 2008 [1], and further played the CCT5 and CCT7.
