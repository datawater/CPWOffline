Eichhörnchen [1]

Eichhörnchen, (Eichhoernchen, белка, belka)
a private chess engine [2] written by Wieland Belka. Eichhörnchen played the First Italian Open Chess Software Cup, 2010 in Carugate and the WCRCC 2019. It further played Olivier Deville's OpenWar and ChessWar tournaments [3].
