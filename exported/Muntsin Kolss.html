Muntsin Kolss [1]

Muntsin Kolss,
a Korean born, German computer scientist and chess programmer. While affiliated with the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology in the 2000s, he researched in the field of computational linguistics on machine translation. Along with his twin brother Munjong, Muntsin is author of the chess engine Ikarus.
