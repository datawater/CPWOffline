Queen,
a WinBoard and UCI compliant chess program with an own GUI by Leen Ammeraal, written in C++, first released in April 2000 [2], the WinBoard version in January 2001 [3]. Queen applies principal variation search utilizing a transposition table, storing both lower and upper bound scores with separate drafts but only one best move [4].
