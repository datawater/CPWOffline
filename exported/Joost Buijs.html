Joost Buijs,
a Dutch computer chess and games programmer, author of the chess program Nightmare, and the Othello program REV, which won the Bronze medal at the 3rd Computer Olympiad 1991 in Maastricht. Joost already started chess programming in fall 1977, when he developed Nightmare on a Heathkit H8 [2]. He further cooperated with Bart Goldhoorn, mentioned as co-author of Goldbar at the DOCCC 2010 [3].
