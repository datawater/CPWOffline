Truman Collins [1]

Truman Wesley Collins, Jr.,
an American computer scientist with an undergraduate degree in mathematics, computer science and economics from Willamette University, coupled with a M.A. in computer science from Stanford University. He is very involved in The Collins Companies where he serves on the board, and the Collins Foundation, as president [2]. During the mid 80s, while affiliated with Willamette University, he wrote the chess program Pawnder as senior project in computer science [3].
