TCEC Season 19,
the Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC) Season 19 from August 06 until October 16, 2020. After 93 games the Superfinal was decided, when Stockfish aka Stockfish NNUE reached 50½ of 100 points of 50 game pairs to defend its TCEC title! In total Stockfish won 18 and lost 9 games, 8 game pairs with both White wins, 10 game pairs in favour to Stockfish - one game pair won by Leela. There was no win by Black.

Superfinal : Stockfish 202009282242_nn-baeb9ef2d183 - LCZero v0.26.3-rc1_T60.SV.JH.92-190 54½-45½
