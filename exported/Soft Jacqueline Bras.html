Cardrona Bra Fence [1]

Soft Jacqueline Bras,
a chess engine by Bruno Bras, apparently derived from Bras' earlier engines Pacque Expert, Chess Christy and Kristy Lenix [2]. Soft Jacqueline Bras played the Massy 2004 and Massy 2005 French Programmers Tournaments, and the FCCC 2004 French Computer Chess Championship.
