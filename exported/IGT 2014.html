Selected Games

CyberPagno was the dedicated tournament winner with a half point ahead of Pedone before the last round, and already announced mate in 9 versus AdaChess. Apparently due to a bug, instead of the winning 48...Nd4, CyberPagno played the disasterous 48...d4 to the same target square and lost the game and the title [3].
