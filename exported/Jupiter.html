Jupiter [1]

Jupiter,
a chess program by Javier Ros Padilla, written in Turbo Pascal. Jupiter participated at the First Spanish Computer Chess Championship 1993 in Barcelona where it finished in the middle of the field with 3 out of 6 and 4th place.
