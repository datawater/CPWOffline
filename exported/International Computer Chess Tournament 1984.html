Home * Tournaments * International Computer Chess Tournament 1984

On May 18, 19, and 20, 1984, a International Computer Chess Tournament had been held, organized by the CSVN. Aegon insurance company sponsored the tournament, which took place in their Training Centre at Baarn, The Netherlands. Six European programs participated.
