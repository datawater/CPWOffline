Warlord,
a chess program by William H. Rogers, written in Basic. Warlord has an own Graphical User Interface, and applies alpha-beta with iterative deepening and evaluation primarily based on material, piece protection, and mobility aka number of legal moves. Warlord Beta V1 played the CCT4 with respectable 5/11.
