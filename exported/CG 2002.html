Evaluation and Learning

  • David Wolfe (2002). Distinguishing Gamblers from Investors at the Blackjack Table. CG 2002

Search

  • Nathan Sturtevant (2002). A Comparison of Algorithms for Multi-player Games. CG 2002
  • Henry Avetisyan, Richard J. Lorentz (2002). Selective Search in an Amazons Program. CG 2002
  • Ingo Althöfer, Raymond Georg Snatzke (2002). Playing Games with Multiple Choice Systems. CG 2002
  • Levente Kocsis, Jos Uiterwijk, Eric Postma, Jaap van den Herik (2002). The Neural MoveMap Heuristic in Chess. CG 2002
  • Reijer Grimbergen, Jeff Rollason (2002). Board Maps and Hill-Climbing for Opening and Middle Game Play in Shogi. CG 2002

Combinatorial Games/Theory

  • Erik D. Demaine, Martin L. Demaine, Rudolf Fleischer (2002). Solitaire Clobber. CG 2002
  • Aviezri Fraenkel, Ofer Rahat (2002). Complexity of Error-Correcting Codes Derived from Combinatorial Games. CG 2002
  • Teigo Nakamura, Elwyn Berlekamp (2002). Analysis of Composite Corridors. CG 2002

Opening/Endgame Databases

Commercial Games

  • Clark Verbrugge (2002). A Structure for Modern Computer Narratives. CG 2002
