Dennis Sceviour,
a Canadian avocational computer chess programmer and author of the Chess Engine Communication Protocol compliant chess engine Schooner, released in 2015 [1]. He already started chess programming in the late 70s, and followed Bob Hyatt's Crafty development during his dormant programming years [2].
