The 2000 Australasian National Computer Chess Championship (NC3) was held from July 15 to 16, 2000, as part of the Australian National University Chess Festival [1] , Red Hill, Canberra, Australia. Free chess server hosted by Andrew Tridgell, organized by Shaun Press.
