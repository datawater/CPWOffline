Moritz Berger [1]

Moritz Berger,
a German computer scientist with a diplom degree in 2000 from University of Bonn, and since 2008 resident in the Seattle area, Washington, until 2013 affiliated with Microsoft. During the 90s Moritz Berger was computer chess aficionado, tester and expert poster in computer chess forums, and in 1997, one of the founders of CCC [2] [3].
