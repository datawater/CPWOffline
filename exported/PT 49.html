Denksportcentrum Leiden [1]

The CSVN 49th Programmers Tournament, took place on October 17-18, 2015, Denksportcentrum Leiden. Jonny won on resistance points tied with Shredder, both 6/7, followed by GridProtector and HIARCS with 5/7.
