Shun-Chin Hsu [1]

Shun-Chin Hsu,
a Taiwanese computer scientiest and Professor at the Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science [2] at the National Taiwan University. His research interests covers Games, specially Chinese Chess and Computer Go, Retrograde Analysis for Chinese Chess Endgame Tablebases, and various search issues. Shun-Chin Hsu is co-author of various strong programs, successfully competing at ICGA Tournaments [3] of Chinese Chess, Go and Connect6. Along with Shi-Jim Yen and Tsan-Cheng Su, Shun-Chin Hsu is author of the Othello program Tower of Othello, which won silver at TAAI 2013 [4] and the gold medal (shared) at TCGA 2014.
