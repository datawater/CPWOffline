Home * Engine Testing * Test-Positions

Note: In recent years testing positions has fallen out of favor among top engine developers. SPRT tests are now generally regarded as a superior method for engine testing.

There are several classical and new developed test-position suites available as

  • Forsyth-Edwards Notation (FEN)
  • Extended Position Description (EPD)
  • Portable Game Notation (PGN)
