Wen-Jie Tseng [1]

Wen-Jie Tseng,
a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu. He is co-author of the Chinese Chess engine Chimo, and further co-author of two programs which won their respective tournaments at the 17th Computer Olympiad in Yokohama 2013 , the Chinese Dark Chess engine DarkKnight [2] , and the Connect6 engine Mobile6 [3] .
