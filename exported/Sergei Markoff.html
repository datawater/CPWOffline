Sergei S. Markoff [1]

Sergei S. Markoff, [2]
a Russian computer scientist, computer chess and games programmer. He is author of the chess program SmarThink [3] [4]. In 2003 Sergei introduced the Botvinnik-Markoff Extension [5]. Sergei working in Sberbank leading a workgroup that focused on development of bank AI/ML systems especially in the area of speech technologies.[6].
