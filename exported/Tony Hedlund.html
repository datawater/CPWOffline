Tony Hedlund [1]

Tony Hedlund, (died early November 2019 [2])
was a Swedish correspondence chess player, International Correspondence Chess Grandmaster, computer chess expert, long term tester and administrator of the SSDF database of test games [3], and author of many articles and game annotations in the Swedish computer chess magazine PLY and subsequently, in his Computer Chess Newsletter. In 2003, Tony Hedlund served as moderator in CCC [4] .
