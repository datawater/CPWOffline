Toma Roncevic [1]

Toma Roncevic, (Toma Rončević)
a Croatian computer scientist and lecturer at Department of Information Technology, University of Split. He graduated from Dipartimento di Informatica, University of Pisa, Italy with focus on artificial intelligence and robotics. As an avid chess player and former chess programmer, Toma is author of the WinBoard compliant chess engine Thor's Hammer aka Mjoelner.
