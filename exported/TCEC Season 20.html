TCEC Season 20,
the Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC) Season 20 from December 01, 2020 until February 01, 2021. Stockfish 20210113 defended its TCEC title! In total Stockfish won 14 and lost 8 games, 4 game pairs with both White wins, 10 game pairs in favour to Stockfish - four game pairs won by Leela.
