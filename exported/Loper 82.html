Loper 82,
a chess program by L. H. Verberkt written 1981 in Basic which played the DOCCC 1982 in Wageningen on a Video Genie with Z80 CPU. Verberkt had predicted that his program would lose all of its games, but nevertheless found it fun to participate [1].
