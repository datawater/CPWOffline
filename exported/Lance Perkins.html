Lance Perkins,
a Canadian professional software developer and as chess programmer initial author of Thinker, whose development was assumed by Kerwin Medina in 2009 [1] [2]. Lance Perkins started chess programming dating back when he first saw Borland's Turbo Chess, and wrote his first private program with an own GUI, and many years later finally decided to bolt a WinBoard interface, submitted Thinker to Leo Dijksman's tournament [3].
