The Red Brick, Tilburg University [1]

The 2nd World Chess Software Championship from November 24 to 26, 2011, Tilburg University, Tilburg [2], The Netherlands in conjunction with the 19th World Computer Chess Championship, the 16th Computer Olympiad and the Advances in Computer Games 13 conference.
