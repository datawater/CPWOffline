Stéphane Nicolet [1]

Stéphane Nicolet,
a French world-class Othello player, two times vice-world champion in 1996 and 2007 [2], computer scientist, teacher and researcher affiliated with University of Paris II Panthéon-Assas [3], and computer Othello and chess programmer. He is primary author of the Othello program Cassio for Macintosh [4]. In 2014, Stéphane Nicolet started to work on Stockfish, and became a primary member of the Stockfish community, contributing in all areas with focus on evaluation, tuning and testing.
