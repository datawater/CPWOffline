Nvidia Corporation, (NVIDIA)
an American multinational technology company incorporated in Delaware and based in Santa Clara, California, founded on April 1993 by Jensen Huang. Nvidia focuses on designing and manufacturing GPUs, supported by their CUDA (Compute Unified Device Architecture) parallel computing API for the purpose of GPGPU.
